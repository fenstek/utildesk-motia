# Rebelle

Rebelle is a digital painting application focused on natural media simulation. It is especially interesting for artists who want realistic watercolour, acrylic and ink effects without leaving a desktop workflow.

Use it when the feel of paint, paper and pigment is more important than broad photo-editing or layout features.
