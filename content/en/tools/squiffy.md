- **AI-powered design suggestions:** Automatic generation of design ideas based on user preferences and current trends.

- **Team collaboration:** Collaborative project work with real-time comments and version control.
- **Templates and assets:** Access to an extensive library of design templates and graphic elements.

- **Cloud-based platform:** Access from anywhere and easy integration with additional tools via APIs.
