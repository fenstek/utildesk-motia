## Who is Smartling suitable for?

Smartling is a good option for organizations where translation management for product text and localization regularly takes time. It is especially worthwhile for SaaS, e-commerce, and content teams working in multiple languages. A clearly responsible owner should accompany the process.

The tool is not ideal if the point of caution remains hard to control: without terminology and review rules, it only produces inconsistent texts faster. In that case, the process should be simplified first before additional software is introduced.
