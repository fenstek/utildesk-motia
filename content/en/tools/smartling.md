# Smartling

Smartling is a cloud-based translation management platform that helps companies translate content efficiently and automatically into different languages. With AI-powered features and automation tools, Smartling streamlines the translation process, increases productivity, and ensures consistent, high-quality localization.

## Who is Smartling suitable for?

Smartling is aimed at companies of all sizes that operate globally or want to offer their products and services internationally. The platform is particularly suitable for:

- Marketing teams creating multilingual campaigns
- Software and web developers who want to localize their applications
- E-commerce companies with an international customer base
- Translation agencies looking to automate workflows
- Content managers seeking centralized control over translations

The platform supports both small teams and large enterprises with complex localization requirements.

## Key features

- **Automated translation management:** Integrated workflows to control and automate the entire translation process.
- **AI-assisted translation:** Use of machine translation followed by human quality review.
- **Content integration:** APIs and plugins for seamless connection to CMSs, apps, websites, and other platforms.
- **Terminology and style guide management:** Consistent use of technical terms and brand style.
- **Real-time translation status:** Transparent overview of translation progress and status.
- **Quality assurance:** Tools for checking consistency, spelling, and formatting.
- **Multilingual project management:** Manage multiple language versions in one dashboard.
- **Team collaboration:** Task assignment, communication, and feedback features within the platform.
- **Reports and analytics:** Detailed evaluations of translation efficiency and quality.

## Pros and cons

### Pros
- Efficient automation reduces manual work and sources of error.
- Integration with many common platforms and tools.
- AI support improves speed and scalability.
- Transparent project overview for better control.
- Flexibility through customizable workflows and terminology management.

### Cons
- Costs can be high depending on scope and number of users.
- Steeper learning curve when setting up and using complex features.
- Dependence on an internet connection and cloud services.
- May be overpowered for very small teams or individual translators.

## Pricing & costs

Smartling offers different pricing plans based on the user's requirements. Costs vary depending on the number of languages, the volume of content, and desired additional services such as machine translation or dedicated support. Standard public pricing is usually not available, as custom quotes are created. Interested users should contact the provider directly to receive a tailored offer.
