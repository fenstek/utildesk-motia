# ArtRage

ArtRage is built for artists who want digital tools to feel closer to traditional painting. Its brushes and canvas behaviour are designed around natural media, making it approachable for sketching, illustration and expressive desktop artwork.

It is a good fit when realistic paint behaviour matters more than a large production suite.
