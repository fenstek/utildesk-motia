# Mailbutler

Mailbutler is an intelligent email productivity tool that integrates seamlessly with common email clients such as Apple Mail, Gmail, and Outlook. It helps users manage their email more efficiently by offering automations, smart tracking options, and other AI-powered features. With Mailbutler, emails can be organized better, appointments can be scheduled, and communication can be personalized - ideal for professionals who work extensively with email marketing and customer communication.

## Who is Mailbutler suitable for?

Mailbutler is mainly aimed at professionals, freelancers, and teams who want to optimize their email communication. It is especially useful for:

- Sales representatives who want to keep track of customer contacts
- Marketing teams that send personalized campaigns by email
- Freelancers and consultants who want to manage their time more efficiently
- Anyone who works a lot with email correspondence and wants to use automations

The tool can be used across platforms and supports different email programs, making it flexible for a variety of work environments.

## Main Features

- **Email tracking:** Track when and where emails are opened
- **Snooze function:** Hide emails from the inbox on a schedule and bring them back later
- **Follow-up reminders:** Automatic reminders if no reply is received to an email
- **Notes and tasks:** Attach notes directly to emails and create to-dos
- **Templates & signatures:** Use professional email templates and personalized signatures
- **Calendar integration:** Schedule appointments and meetings directly from email
- **Automations:** Optimize workflows by automating recurring actions
- **AI-powered features:** Smart suggestions for wording and better phrasing
- **Team features:** Shared use of templates, notes, and contacts within the team
