# Auferet

Auferet is an AI game master for solo text adventures and tabletop-style RPGs. You describe what you do, and a language model runs the world around you, narrating scenes, voicing characters, and resolving outcomes. What sets it apart is persistence: it keeps a long-term memory of your story and lets you upload your own lore documents, so the setting stays consistent across long sessions instead of drifting.

## Who is Auferet suitable for?

Auferet suits solo roleplayers, interactive-fiction readers, and tabletop fans who want a game master on demand. Bring your own world by uploading lore, or start from a prompt and let the AI build outward. It runs in the browser and as a published Android app, with a free daily tier and paid plans that lift the daily limit and use stronger models.
