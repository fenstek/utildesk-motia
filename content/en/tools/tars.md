# Tars

Tars is an AI-powered chatbot creation platform that helps businesses run automated, interactive conversations with customers. The solution makes it possible to create custom chatbots without programming knowledge, and these can be used across different industries for customer service, lead generation, and business process automation.

## Who is Tars suitable for?

Tars is aimed primarily at companies and teams that want to automate customer communication to save time and resources. The tool is especially suitable for marketing departments, sales organizations, customer service teams, and small to medium-sized businesses that want to implement interactive chatbot solutions without major technical effort. Agencies that want to offer chatbot services to their clients also benefit from the platform.

## Main features

- **Drag-and-drop chatbot builder:** Create chatbots without programming knowledge through visual design.
- **Template library:** Access ready-made chatbot templates for various use cases.
- **Third-party integrations:** Connect with CRM systems, email marketing tools, payment providers, and more.
- **User and conversation analytics:** Evaluate chat interactions to optimize bot performance.
- **Multilingual support:** Support for multiple languages for global use.
- **Lead generation and qualification:** Automatically collect and score leads through chat.
- **Cross-platform use:** Deploy on websites, mobile apps, and messaging services.
- **Automated replies:** Quickly respond to common customer inquiries with AI-powered answers.
- **Custom workflows:** Design individualized conversation flows for different scenarios.
