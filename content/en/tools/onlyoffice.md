# OnlyOffice

OnlyOffice is a versatile platform that combines collaborative office tools with automation and AI features. It enables efficient creation, editing, and management of documents, spreadsheets, and presentations in real time. OnlyOffice is aimed at businesses and teams that want to increase productivity through intelligent automation and seamless collaboration.

## Who is OnlyOffice suitable for?

OnlyOffice is especially well suited for small to medium-sized businesses, educational institutions, and freelancers looking for an integrated solution for document management and teamwork. The platform is ideal for users who value data privacy and want to manage their data both locally and in the cloud. In addition, teams that want to use automated workflows and AI-powered assistant features benefit from the available functions.

## Key Features

- **Online document editing:** Create and collaboratively edit text documents, spreadsheets, and presentations in real time.
- **AI-powered assistants:** Automated suggestions for writing, proofreading, and formatting text.
- **Workflow automation:** Integration of scripts and automation rules to optimize repetitive tasks.
- **Multi-user access:** Management of access rights and team collaboration.
- **Compatibility:** Support for Microsoft Office formats (DOCX, XLSX, PPTX) and other common file types.
- **Integration:** Connection to cloud services, CRM systems, and other business tools.
- **Data privacy:** Options for local installation and data control.
- **Mobile apps:** Access to and editing of documents on mobile devices.

## Pricing & Costs

OnlyOffice offers different pricing models based on the required features and number of users. There are both free basic versions with limited functionality and paid plans with advanced features such as expanded automation, support, and cloud hosting. Prices may vary depending on the provider and license type. For detailed information, it is advisable to consult the official website.

## What really matters in daily use

OnlyOffice is interesting for teams that want Office-compatible editing with more control over hosting and integration. The decisive questions are file fidelity, collaborative editing, permissions, and whether existing storage or collaboration systems can be connected cleanly.

## Workflow Fit

- Good for organizations with Nextcloud, self-hosting, or data-protection requirements that still need familiar document formats.
- Less suitable when maximum Microsoft 365 integration, macro compatibility, or very complex Office templates are decisive.

## Editorial Assessment

OnlyOffice is a serious alternative when control and format proximity matter more than a full enterprise ecosystem. Demanding documents should be tested with real files before rollout.
