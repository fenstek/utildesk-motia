# RightInbox

RightInbox is a powerful tool for boosting email management productivity, designed specifically for Gmail users. It integrates seamlessly into the Gmail inbox and offers automated follow-up reminders, email scheduling, and other smart features that make email communication more efficient.

## Who is RightInbox suitable for?

RightInbox is aimed at professionals, sales teams, freelancers, and anyone who manages a large number of emails every day and wants to save time. The tool is especially useful for people who frequently need to send follow-ups or want emails to be sent automatically at specific times. It can also be a valuable support for teams that want to structure and optimize their communication.

## Key features

- **Automatic follow-ups:** Reminders for unanswered emails are set up automatically so that no important messages are forgotten.
- **Email scheduling:** Emails can be written in advance and sent automatically at a later time.
- **Templates:** Create and reuse email templates to speed up correspondence.
- **Email tracking:** Notifications when a sent email has been opened or clicked.
- **Notes:** Add internal notes to emails that are only visible to the user.
- **Integration:** Works as an extension directly in Gmail and is compatible with common browsers.
- **Snooze function:** Emails can be temporarily hidden and shown again at a later time.

## Pros and cons

### Pros

- Easy integration into Gmail without additional software installation
- Saves time through automated follow-ups and scheduling
- Helps organize and track email communication better
- Flexible usage options thanks to the different features in the tool
- Clearly supports productivity in day-to-day work

### Cons

- Features are limited to Gmail and are not available for other email clients
- Some advanced features are only included in paid plans
- Depends on a stable internet connection and Gmail service availability
- Privacy concerns may play a role depending on company policy

RightInbox offers different pricing plans that vary depending on the feature set and usage period. Exact prices depend on the provider and the plan selected.

## FAQ

**1. Does RightInbox only work with Gmail?**  
Yes, RightInbox is designed specifically for Gmail and works as a browser extension that integrates directly into the Gmail inbox.

**2. Is there a free version of RightInbox?**  
There is a free basic plan with limited features, ideal for testing. A paid subscription is required for advanced features.

**3. Can I use RightInbox to send emails automatically at specific times?**  
Yes, one of the core features is email scheduling, which lets you send messages automatically at a desired time.

**4. Is my data processed securely when using RightInbox?**  
RightInbox places importance on privacy and security, but users should still review the relevant privacy policies, especially when handling sensitive company data.

**5. Is RightInbox suitable for teams as well?**  
Yes, there are team plans that offer features for sharing templates, tracking, and collaboration.

**6. Which browsers are supported?**  
RightInbox is compatible with common browsers such as Google Chrome, Firefox, and Microsoft Edge.

**7. Can I create and save email templates with RightInbox?**  
Yes, the tool allows you to create, save, and reuse templates, making it faster to send frequently used emails.

**8. Is there a mobile app for RightInbox?**  
RightInbox is currently mainly available as a browser extension; there is no standalone mobile app.
