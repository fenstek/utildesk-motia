# Leonardo AI

Leonardo AI is an innovative artificial intelligence platform specializing in the creation and editing of images using AI technologies. With a user-friendly interface and versatile features, Leonardo AI enables both beginners and professionals to generate high-quality visual content with ease. The tool is especially suitable for creative projects, design work, and visual communication, and its freemium pricing model allows for flexible use.

Leonardo AI is aimed at a broad audience, including:

- Designers and artists who want to use AI-powered tools for image generation and editing
- Marketing and advertising professionals who want to create visual content for campaigns quickly
- Developers and tech enthusiasts who want to experiment with AI technologies
- Small and medium-sized businesses that want to produce creative content cost-effectively
