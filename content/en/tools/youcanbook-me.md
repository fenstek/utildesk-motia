# YouCanBook.me

YouCanBook.me is an intelligent online scheduling tool that automates and simplifies appointment booking. It lets users share calendars with clients or colleagues so appointments can be booked easily without email back-and-forth or phone calls. Through integration with common calendar services and customization to fit individual needs, YouCanBook.me supports efficient scheduling.

## Who is YouCanBook.me suitable for?

YouCanBook.me is aimed at individuals, freelancers, small and medium-sized businesses, and teams that want to optimize their scheduling. The tool is especially useful for consultants, coaches, service providers, and support teams that need to coordinate regular client or customer appointments. Educational institutions or organizations with a high volume of appointments also benefit from the automation and ease of use.

## Key Features

- **Automatic appointment booking:** Syncs with calendars such as Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook to show available times in real time.
- **Customizable booking pages:** Option to tailor the design and questions on the booking page individually.
- **Time zone detection:** Automatically adjusts the offered times to the booker's time zone.
- **Notifications & reminders:** Automatically sends email or SMS reminders to both parties.
- **Integration with other tools:** Compatible with CRM systems, payment providers, and video conferencing services.
- **Group and team scheduling:** Manages multiple calendars and availabilities within a team.
- **Multiple booking types:** Supports one-on-one appointments, group bookings, or recurring appointments.
- **Reports and analytics:** Overview of bookings and user behavior to optimize appointment management.
- **Mobile optimization:** Access and manage appointments on mobile devices.
