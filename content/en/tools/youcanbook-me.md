If the issue is appointment booking with calendar sync and availability management, the tool can bring order and help organize booking links, rules, and buffer times without endless email back-and-forth.

## Key features

- **Customizable booking pages:** Ability to tailor the design and questions on the booking page individually.
- **Time zone detection:** Automatically adjusts offered times to the booker's time zone.
- **Notifications & reminders:** Automatic sending of email or SMS reminders to both parties.
- **Integration with other tools:** Compatibility with CRM systems, payment providers, and video conferencing services.
- **Group and team scheduling:** Management of multiple calendars and availability within a team.
- **Multiple booking types:** Support for one-on-one appointments, group bookings, or recurring appointments.
- **Reports and analytics:** Overview of bookings and user behavior to optimize appointment management.
- **Mobile optimization:** Access to and management of appointments via mobile devices.

- **Practical check:** which calendars, time zones, and pre-questions are really necessary.
- **Team rollout:** organizing booking links, rules, and buffer times without endless email back-and-forth.
