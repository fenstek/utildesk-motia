# Domo

Domo is a cloud-based platform for data analysis and business intelligence that utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) to support businesses in making data-driven decisions. With a combination of data integration, visualization, and AI-driven analytics, Domo enables the easy integration of various data sources and provides valuable insights in real-time.

## For Who is Domo Suitable?

Domo is suitable for any size of business that wants to centralize and intelligently utilize its data. It is particularly well-suited for:

- Companies seeking an all-in-one solution for data integration, analysis, and reporting
- Teams that rely on real-time data and AI-driven insights
- Departments such as Sales, Marketing, Finance, and IT that want to make data-driven decisions
- Organizations that prefer scalable and customizable business intelligence tools

## Key Features

- **Data Integration:** Connection to hundreds of data sources, including cloud services, databases, and on-premises systems
- **Real-Time Dashboards:** Customizable dashboards with up-to-date data visualizations for fast decision-making
- **AI-Driven Analytics:** Utilization of machine learning and artificial intelligence for pattern recognition and prediction
- **Automated Reporting:** Creation and distribution of reports based on predefined rules
- **Mobile App:** Access to data and dashboards from anywhere using mobile devices
- **Collaboration:** Joint work on data projects and dashboards within the team
- **Scalability:** Adaptable to growing data volumes and user numbers
- **Security Features:** Data protection and access controls for sensitive company data
