# ToonMe

ToonMe is an innovative mobile application that lets users transform photos into cartoon- or comic-style images with the help of artificial intelligence. The app uses advanced algorithms to automatically recognize portraits and convert them into a range of artistic styles. ToonMe is especially popular with users who want to create creative, personalized profile pictures or artwork from their photos.

## Who is ToonMe for?

ToonMe is aimed at anyone who enjoys creatively editing photos and wants to turn their images into unique cartoon or comic formats. The app is especially suitable for:

- Social media users who want eye-catching profile pictures or posts.
- Artists and designers looking for inspiration or quick transformations.
- Users without technical experience, since the app is easy and intuitive to use.
- People who prefer a mobile solution for image editing.
- Fans of AI-based tools who want to experiment with modern technology.

## Main Features

- **Automatic cartoonization:** Quickly and automatically turns photos into different cartoon or comic styles.
- **Wide range of filters and effects:** Choose from multiple artistic styles, including classic cartoons, digital art, and more.
- **Portrait recognition:** AI-based face detection for precise and realistic transformations.
- **Mobile use:** Available as an app for iOS and Android, ideal for editing on the go.
- **Easy to use:** User-friendly interface that requires no prior knowledge.
- **Sharing function:** Allows direct export and sharing of created images on social networks.
- **Regular updates:** New styles and features are added regularly.
- **Freemium model:** Core features are free to use, with advanced features available through a subscription.
