# SummarizeBot

SummarizeBot is an AI-powered tool that helps you quickly and accurately summarize long texts, meetings, or conversations. It uses advanced algorithms to extract the most important information and present it in a compact format. This saves users valuable time and helps them focus on what matters most.

## Who is SummarizeBot suitable for?

SummarizeBot is ideal for professionals, students, project managers, and anyone who regularly works with extensive texts or meetings. The tool is especially useful for teams that want to document their meetings and quickly access the key points. It is also valuable for people who want to reduce information overload and keep track of everything.

## Main Features

- Automatic summarization of texts, documents, and web pages
- Real-time transcription and summarization of meetings and conversations
- Integration with common chat and collaboration tools
- Adjustable summary length (short, medium, detailed)
- Support for multiple languages
- Export function for summaries in various formats (PDF, TXT, DOCX)
- Privacy-focused processing without storing sensitive data (depending on the provider)
- User-friendly interface and easy operation
