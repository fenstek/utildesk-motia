# Hatchful by Shopify

Hatchful by Shopify is a free online tool for creating logos and branding materials with ease. It is aimed especially at small businesses, startups, and individuals who want to design a professional-looking logo without any design experience. With a user-friendly interface and numerous templates, Hatchful makes it possible to create designs quickly and easily that can be used directly for marketing purposes.

## Who is Hatchful by Shopify suitable for?

Hatchful is particularly well suited for founders, small business owners, and freelancers who need an attractive logo without paying high design costs. The tool is also practical for marketing teams that want to quickly test different logo variations. Since no prior knowledge is required, Hatchful is ideal for anyone who wants to develop their own branding in a straightforward and time-saving way.

## Key Features

- **Varied design templates:** Choose from numerous prebuilt logo styles suitable for different industries and target groups.
- **Easy customization:** Colors, fonts, symbols, and layouts can be edited intuitively.
- **Automatic generation:** After entering a company name and industry, the tool automatically creates suitable logo drafts.
- **Branding packages:** In addition to logos, matching social media graphics and business cards can also be created.
- **Downloads in various formats:** Logos are available in several file types, such as PNG, which is ideal for web and print.
- **Cloud storage:** Drafts can be saved online and edited at any time.
- **Free to use:** All core features are available at no cost.

No, Hatchful is specifically designed for users without design experience.
