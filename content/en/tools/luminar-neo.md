# Luminar Neo

Luminar Neo is a modern photo editing software that stands out especially for its AI-powered features. It is aimed at creatives who want to enhance their photos quickly and professionally without relying on complex editing workflows. The software offers an intuitive user interface and powerful tools that are suitable for both beginners and experienced photographers.

## Who is Luminar Neo suitable for?

Luminar Neo is suitable for hobby photographers, content creators, designers, and professional photographers who value efficient and creative image editing. It is particularly a good solution for users who want to benefit from automation through artificial intelligence. The software is designed for desktop users and supports both Windows and macOS. Anyone looking for a flexible and modern alternative to traditional photo editing programs will find Luminar Neo an interesting option.

## Key features

- **AI-powered image enhancement:** Automatic optimization of exposure, colors, and details.
- **Background removal:** Easy cutout of subjects with just a few clicks.
- **Sky replacement:** Creative replacement of the sky with realistic light adjustment.
- **Portrait retouching:** Tools for skin smoothing, eye brightening, and facial correction.
- **Layers and masks:** Advanced editing options through a flexible layer structure.
- **Batch processing:** Fast editing of multiple images at the same time.
- **Plug-in support:** Integration into existing workflows with other programs.
- **RAW support:** Direct editing of RAW files with high quality.
- **User-friendly interface:** Intuitive operation even for beginners.
- **Regular updates:** New features and improvements through the subscription model.
