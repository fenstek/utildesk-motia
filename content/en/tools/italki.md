# italki

italki is an online platform that connects language learners with qualified teachers from all over the world. Through italki, users can book individually tailored language lessons designed to meet their specific needs and goals. The platform operates as a marketplace for language teachers of various languages and allows for flexible, location-independent learning via video chat.

## Who is italki suitable for?

italki is aimed at anyone who wants to learn a new language or improve their existing language skills—regardless of their level. italki is especially suitable for:

- Learners who prefer personal lessons and want to remain flexible.
- Individuals learning languages for professional or personal reasons.
- Students looking to focus on pronunciation, grammar, or conversation.
- Advanced learners aiming to intensify their language practice with native speakers.
- Travelers seeking to prepare quickly for a new language.

The platform is open to all age groups and proficiency levels since teachers with different specializations and teaching styles are available.

## Main Features

- **Wide selection of language teachers:** Hundreds of teachers from around the world offer lessons in many languages.
- **Customizable lessons:** Users can choose teachers, lesson duration, and content according to their needs.
- **Usage-based pricing:** Costs depend on the chosen teacher and lesson length.
- **Video chat lessons:** Interactive lessons using integrated video and audio features.
- **Language exchange community:** In addition to paid lessons, free language exchange opportunities are available.
- **Rating system:** Users can rate teachers and read reviews to find suitable instructors.
- **Mobile app:** Flexible learning on the go with the italki app for iOS and Android.
- **Calendar and scheduling management:** Easy booking and management of lessons.
- **Language learning resources:** Access to learning materials and exercises provided by teachers.

## Advantages and Disadvantages

### Advantages

- Large selection of teachers from various countries and cultures.
- Flexible booking based on personal schedule and budget.
- Personalized instruction tailored to individual needs.
- Opportunity to converse with native speakers and learn cultural aspects.
- No long-term contracts or subscriptions required.
- Combination of paid lessons and free language exchange.
- User-friendly and clearly structured platform.

### Disadvantages

- Teacher quality and experience can vary due to the open marketplace.
- Lesson prices can vary widely depending on teacher and language.
- No standardized curricula—lesson content varies by teacher.
- Internet connection and technical setup can affect lesson quality.
- Beginners may find it challenging to select suitable teachers and courses.

## Pricing & Costs

italki uses a usage-based pricing model. Users pay only for the lessons they book. Prices vary depending on the teacher, language, and lesson duration. Typically, costs range from approximately 5 to 30 Euros per hour. Some teachers also offer shorter sessions or trial lessons at reduced prices.

There are no monthly subscriptions or hidden fees. Payment is made directly through the platform before each lesson. italki also offers credit accounts that allow users to manage their lesson budgets.

## Alternatives to italki

- **Preply:** Also a marketplace for language lessons with many teachers and a usage-based pricing model.
- **Tandem:** Focuses on free language exchange combined with paid tutors.
- **Verbling:** Platform for online language lessons with video chat and professional teachers.
- **Lingoda:** Offers structured courses and live lessons on a subscription basis.
- **HelloTalk:** Language learning app with community and language exchange focus, less suited for formal lessons.

## FAQ

**1. Do I need prior knowledge to use italki?**
No, italki is suitable for all language levels—from absolute beginners to advanced learners.

**2. How do I find the right teacher?**
You can filter by language, price, availability, and ratings to find a teacher that fits your learning goals.

**3. How do I pay for lessons?**
Payment is made in advance through the platform via credit card, PayPal, or other payment methods, depending on the provider.

**4. Can I get a free trial lesson?**
Many teachers offer trial lessons at reduced prices, but free lessons are rare.

**5. Is there a minimum contract period?**
No, italki has no minimum commitments—you only book the lessons you need.

**6. How does the lesson work technically?**
Lessons take place via italki's platform using video chat. A stable internet connection is important.

**7. Can I cancel or reschedule lessons?**
Yes, cancellation and rescheduling policies depend on the individual teacher and are available before booking.

**8. Is there an italki app?**
Yes, italki offers a mobile app for iOS and Android, enabling learning on the go.
