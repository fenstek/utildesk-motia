VanceAI Image Enlarger is aimed at users who want to enlarge images without sacrificing quality. This includes:

- Graphic designers who need to scale smaller images for projects.

- Social media managers who want to publish content in better quality.
- Private users who want to enhance old or low-resolution photos.

The tool is easy to use and requires no prior image editing knowledge, making it attractive to both beginners and professionals.
