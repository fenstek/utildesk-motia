# AI Dungeon

AI Dungeon is an innovative text-based adventure game powered by artificial intelligence. It lets users experience interactive stories in real time and create their own adventures. By using advanced language models, AI Dungeon dynamically generates storylines that adapt flexibly to players’ input. The result is a unique, personalized gameplay experience that goes far beyond traditional text adventures.

## Who is AI Dungeon suitable for?

AI Dungeon is designed for anyone who enjoys creative stories and interactive experiences. The tool is especially suitable for:

- Players who want to experience individualized adventures without being limited to preset storylines.
- Writers and creatives looking for inspiration for their own stories or wanting to test narrative ideas.
- Users interested in artificial intelligence and its application in interactive entertainment.
- People who enjoy role-playing games but prefer flexible and unpredictable scenarios.

The tool is suitable for both beginners and experienced users who enjoy innovative, AI-driven storytelling formats.

## Main features

- **Interactive text adventures:** Experience dynamic stories that adapt to your input.
- **AI-driven story generation:** The AI continuously creates new scenarios based on user actions.
- **Diverse genres:** Choose from different themes such as fantasy, sci-fi, mystery, or create your own settings.
- **Multiplayer mode:** Play together with friends and build adventures collaboratively.
- **Custom worlds:** Create your own characters, places, and rules for your adventure.
- **Save function:** Save and load game progress so you can continue at any time.
- **Voice control:** In some versions, input by voice is possible.
- **Regular updates:** Continuous improvement and expansion of the AI models and features.
- **Community integration:** Access adventures and stories created by other users.
