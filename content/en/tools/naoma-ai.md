# Naoma AI

Naoma AI is an AI video sales agent for B2B SaaS teams. It addresses a clear bottleneck: prospects want to see a product immediately, but a sales team cannot run every demo personally around the clock. Naoma is designed to show interactive product demos to website visitors, qualify leads and route the next step to a CRM, calendar or checkout flow.

## Who is Naoma AI for?

Naoma AI is aimed at SaaS startups, growth teams, product marketing, sales and RevOps. It is especially relevant when a product needs explanation and many early demo requests come in. For highly customised enterprise deals, it does not replace an account executive, but it can improve early qualification and handoff.

## Common use cases

- Offer visitors a guided product demo immediately.
- Qualify leads by use case, company size or buying intent.
- Standardise repetitive first-demo flows.
- Vary demos for different audiences or industries.
- Route prospects into CRM, calendar or checkout.

## Strengths

The main advantage is speed. A prospect does not need to wait for a sales rep; they can experience the product story immediately. At the same time, the agent can collect context: which feature matters, which objections appear and which next step fits. That can make the handoff to sales more concrete.

## Limits

An AI demo is only as good as the positioning, script and source material behind it. Teams need to maintain allowed claims, pricing logic, integration details, roadmap boundaries and escalation to humans. Otherwise the demo may look modern while creating wrong expectations.

Naoma AI offers pricing and sales paths. The relevant plan depends on demo volume, integrations and team size.
