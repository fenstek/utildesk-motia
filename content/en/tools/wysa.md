# Wysa

Wysa is an AI-powered chatbot designed specifically to help people manage mental health challenges and support their emotional well-being. The app combines evidence-based therapeutic techniques with artificial intelligence to offer users anonymous, accessible self-help around the clock. Wysa is increasingly being used as a complementary tool in healthcare and mental health settings.

## Who is Wysa suitable for?

Wysa is aimed at people looking for support with stress, anxiety, depression, or other mental health burdens, but who may not have direct access to professional therapy or want to supplement it. The app is ideal for people who:

- prefer anonymous, low-threshold support  
- want to actively support their mental health on their own  
- want to strengthen their resilience through daily exercises and conversations  
- are looking for additional support alongside traditional forms of therapy  
- need quick support during periods of emotional strain  

Since Wysa is not a substitute for professional psychotherapy, users with severe mental illnesses or acute crises should also seek specialist help.

## Main Features

- **AI-powered chatbot:** Interactive conversations based on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and other therapeutic methods  
- **Guided exercises:** Breathing techniques, mindfulness exercises, journaling functions, and mood trackers  
- **Personalized support:** Content adapted to individual needs and mood states  
- **Anonymity:** No need to provide personal data, helping protect privacy  
- **Resources and tools:** Access to articles, tips, and self-help strategies  
- **24/7 availability:** Usable at any time, with no waiting periods or appointments  
- **Integration with professional help:** Option to receive recommendations for professional support if needed  

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Low-threshold access to mental health support  
- Anonymity and privacy are a priority  
- Scientifically grounded therapeutic approaches  
- Flexible and available at any time, ideal for everyday use  
- Free basic version with many features  
- Regular updates and improvements to the AI  

### Cons

- Not a replacement for professional psychotherapy in severe cases  
- Limited human interaction, since it is AI-based  
- Some features are only available in the paid version  
- Depends on access to a smartphone or internet connection  
- No individual diagnosis or treatment  

## Pricing & Costs

Wysa offers a **Freemium** model. The basic version is free and includes many useful features for getting started with mental health support. For advanced features such as special coaching programs or personal access to human coaches, a paid subscription is required. Exact pricing varies by plan and region.

## Alternatives to Wysa

- **Woebot:** AI chatbot focused on cognitive behavioral therapy, also with a freemium model  
- **Youper:** AI-based app for emotional support and mood analysis  
- **Replika:** Personal AI companion for conversations and emotional support  
- **Moodpath:** Digital companion for identifying depression, with additional therapeutic exercises  
- **Sanvello:** Combination of self-help tools, coaching, and community support  

## FAQ

**1. Is Wysa a replacement for a therapist?**  
No, Wysa is designed as a complementary tool for mental health and does not replace professional psychotherapy or medical treatment.

**2. How secure is my data with Wysa?**  
Wysa places great importance on privacy and anonymity. Personal data is not shared without consent, and communication is confidential.

**3. Which languages does Wysa support?**  
Wysa is available in several languages, including English and others, depending on the version and platform.

**4. Do I need an internet connection to use Wysa?**  
An internet connection is required to use most features, especially the AI chatbot.

**5. Is there a free version of Wysa?**  
Yes, Wysa's basic version is free to use and offers many features for mental health support.

**6. Can Wysa help with severe mental illnesses?**  
Wysa can be supportive, but it is not suitable for treating severe mental illnesses. In such cases, professional help should be sought.

**7. Which devices is Wysa available on?**  
Wysa is available as an app for smartphones (iOS and Android) and can also be used in some cases via web browsers.

**8. How can I subscribe to or upgrade Wysa?**  
Within the app, there are options to upgrade to paid plans with expanded features. Pricing and availability depend on the specific offer.
