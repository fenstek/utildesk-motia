# Setmore

Setmore is an intelligent online appointment scheduling tool that enables businesses and freelancers to manage appointments easily and automate customer bookings. With a user-friendly interface and AI-powered features, Setmore helps reduce administrative effort and makes the booking process more convenient for customers.

## Who is Setmore suitable for?

Setmore is primarily aimed at small and medium-sized businesses, freelancers, coaches, service providers in the health and wellness sector, and teams that want to digitize and optimize their appointment scheduling. It is suitable for users looking for a flexible, cloud-based solution to manage client appointments, offer bookings online, and automate workflows. Companies with multiple employees also benefit from the team management features.

## Key features

- Online appointment booking with calendar integration (Google, Outlook, iCal)
- Multiple calendars for teams and employee management
- Automatic reminders by email and SMS to reduce no-shows
- Customizable booking pages and embedding options on websites and social media
- Payment processing and integration with payment providers (depending on the plan)
- Mobile apps for iOS and Android to manage appointments on the go
- Integration with video conferencing tools such as Zoom or telemedicine platforms
- Reporting and analytics features for evaluating appointment data
- Support for multiple time zones and languages
- API access for custom extensions and automations

## Pros and cons

### Pros
- Intuitive and easy-to-understand user interface
- Extensive features included even in the free version
- Flexibility through various integrations and customization options
- Efficient automation of appointment confirmations and reminders
- Suitable for individuals and teams of different sizes

### Cons
- Some advanced features are only available in paid plans
- Limited interface customization compared with specialized tools

## Pricing & costs

Setmore offers a free basic version with essential features for individual users. For advanced features such as team calendars, SMS reminders, and payment processing, there are various paid plans, with prices varying depending on the provider and chosen package. Monthly subscriptions with different feature sets are typically available and geared toward the needs of small teams through to larger organizations.

## Alternatives to Setmore

## FAQ

**1. Is Setmore free to use?**  
Yes, Setmore offers a free basic version with limited features that is often sufficient for individual users.

**2. Can payments be accepted directly with Setmore?**  
Payment features are included in certain paid plans and allow integration with payment providers.

**3. Which calendars can be integrated?**  
Setmore supports Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCal, among others, for easy synchronization.

**4. Are there mobile apps for Setmore?**  
Yes, Setmore provides apps for iOS and Android that make it possible to manage appointments on the go.

**5. How does appointment booking work for customers?**  
Customers can select and book appointments through a customizable booking page or embedded widgets on websites.

**6. Can teams manage multiple calendars?**  
Yes, Setmore allows the management of multiple employee calendars and the assignment of appointments to different people.

**7. Which languages does Setmore support?**  
The platform is multilingual and supports different languages depending on the region.

**8. How can I contact Setmore support?**  
Support is available by email, chat, or phone depending on the plan; details vary by the selected package.
