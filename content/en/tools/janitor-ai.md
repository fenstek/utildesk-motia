With Janitor AI, it is worth taking a sober look at the reality behind the promise. At its core are character chats and conversation-oriented AI experiences; the tool becomes truly useful when it helps you quickly test roleplay, companion, and community dialogues.

Before introducing it, the question should be answered: which personas, content, and safeguards are acceptable? Otherwise, the value remains difficult to measure. The most important caution point: with unclear safety rules, it can generate problematic content or expectations.

## Who is Janitor AI suitable for?

Janitor AI is a good option for organizations where character chats and conversation-oriented AI experiences regularly consume time. It is especially worth adopting for experimental chat formats with clear moderation and usage limits. A clearly assigned owner should guide the process.

The tool is not ideal if the caution point remains hard to control: with unclear safety rules, it can generate problematic content or expectations. In that case, the workflow should be simplified first before additional software is introduced.
