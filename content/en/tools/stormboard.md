# Stormboard

Stormboard is a digital whiteboard tool designed specifically for team collaboration. It makes it easy to visualize ideas, run brainstorming sessions, and manage projects in an interactive online environment. With real-time collaboration features, Stormboard helps teams work more productively and stay organized, whether they are working together in the office or remotely.

## Who is Stormboard suitable for?

Stormboard is aimed at teams and organizations of any size that value efficient collaboration and creative idea generation. The tool is especially suitable for:

- Project managers and product teams who want to visualize and coordinate their work.
- Creative teams that want to carry out brainstorming and idea gathering digitally.
- Educational institutions that want to design interactive lessons.
- Companies with distributed or remote employees that need a central platform for exchange.

Thanks to its flexible usage options, Stormboard can be adapted to different industries and ways of working.

## Key features

- **Digital whiteboards:** Create interactive boards with notes, drawings, images, and videos.
- **Real-time collaboration:** Multiple users can work at the same time and track changes live.
- **Template library:** Access numerous templates for brainstorming, project planning, SWOT analyses, and more.
- **Task management:** Assign tasks, set priorities, and define deadlines within the boards.
- **Tool integrations:** Connect to common applications such as Microsoft Teams, Slack, and Google Workspace.
- **Comment function:** Direct communication and feedback on individual elements on the board.
- **Reporting:** Export boards and reports for documentation and sharing.
- **Access rights:** Manage user roles and permissions for secure collaboration.

## Pros and cons

### Pros

- Intuitive user interface that is easy to understand even for beginners.
- Encourages creative collaboration and visual organization of ideas.
- Supports distributed teams with real-time updates and cloud access.
- Extensive templates and integration options.
- Flexible task management within the platform.

### Cons

- Some advanced features are only available in paid plans.
- The large number of features can initially feel overwhelming for some users.
- Offline use is limited because the tool primarily works in the cloud.
- Performance can occasionally be affected on very large boards with many users.

## Pricing & costs

Stormboard offers different pricing plans that can vary depending on the feature set and team size. There is usually a free basic version with limited features that is suitable for individuals or small teams. Paid plans offer expanded features such as more storage, additional integrations, improved security options, and priority support.

Exact pricing depends on the chosen plan and possibly on the contract term. For current and detailed information, it is recommended to visit the official website or contact the provider directly.

## Alternatives to Stormboard

- **Miro:** A comprehensive online whiteboard with many integrations and a large user base.
- **Microsoft Whiteboard:** A free tool, especially suitable for users in the Microsoft ecosystem.
- **Conceptboard:** Focuses on visual collaboration and project management with extensive features.
- **Jamboard:** Google's own whiteboard, ideal for Google Workspace users.
- **Lucidspark:** A collaborative whiteboard with strong brainstorming and planning features.

## FAQ

**1. Is Stormboard also suitable for individuals?**  
Yes, there is a free version that is also suitable for individuals, although with limited features.

**2. Can I use Stormboard on mobile devices?**  
Stormboard is accessible via web browsers and offers apps for mobile devices so you can work on the go.

**3. How secure is my data with Stormboard?**  
Stormboard places importance on data security and offers features such as encrypted connections and access controls, but details depend on the chosen plan.

**4. Is there a way to connect Stormboard with other tools?**  
Yes, Stormboard supports integrations with various platforms such as Microsoft Teams, Slack, and Google Workspace.

**5. Can I export my Stormboard boards?**  
Boards and content can be exported in various formats so they can be saved offline or shared.

**6. How many users can work on a board at the same time?**  
The number of simultaneous users can vary depending on the plan, but in general many users can work at the same time.

**7. Does Stormboard offer templates for specific use cases?**  
Yes, there is a wide selection of templates for different purposes such as brainstorming, project planning, or SWOT analyses.

**8. Is there a trial period for the paid plans?**  
In general, Stormboard offers a trial period or demo version so you can try the advanced features before buying.
