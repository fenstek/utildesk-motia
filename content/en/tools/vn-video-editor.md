# VN Video Editor

VN Video Editor is a user-friendly and powerful video editing app available on both mobile devices and desktop computers. With a clean interface and versatile features, it makes it easy for beginners and advanced users alike to create and edit creative, professional videos.

## Who is VN Video Editor for?

VN Video Editor is aimed at anyone who wants to edit videos easily and flexibly. The app is especially suitable for:

- Hobby filmmakers and content creators who prefer mobile video editing
- Social media users who want to create engaging clips quickly
- Beginners who want to get started without complex software
- Advanced users who also want extensive features on mobile devices
- Small businesses and influencers who produce video content regularly

## Key features

- Multi-track video editing with simple timeline control
- Support for HD and 4K videos
- Extensive selection of transitions, effects, and filters
- Text and title editing with customizable fonts and animations
- Music and sound effect integration with a built-in media library
- Keyframe animations for precise motion control
- Speed settings including slow motion and time-lapse
- Export in various resolutions and formats
- Direct sharing to social networks possible
- Cloud synchronization across different devices (depending on the platform)
