# Zeplin

Zeplin is a collaborative design tool built specifically for designers and developers to bring design projects to life more efficiently. It connects design software with the development environment by automatically generating and providing design specifications, assets, and style guides. This reduces misunderstandings between design and development teams and speeds up the development process.

## Who is Zeplin suitable for?

Zeplin is especially well suited for teams working on digital products such as web and mobile applications. Designers who work with tools like Sketch, Adobe XD, or Figma benefit from the ability to move their designs seamlessly into a developer-friendly environment. Developers get access to precise specifications, code snippets, and assets without having to extract data manually. Project managers and product owners can also track progress more easily through the central platform.

## Main features

- **Automatic specifications:** Zeplin extracts measurements, colors, fonts, and spacing from design files and presents them clearly.
- **Asset export:** Automatically exports all necessary graphics in the appropriate formats for development.
- **Style guides:** Generates consistent style guides that document design and development standards.
- **Team collaboration:** Allows comments directly in the design to communicate feedback and changes effectively.
- **Integration with design tools:** Supports Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, and Photoshop for a smooth workflow.
- **Code snippets:** Provides CSS, iOS, and Android code snippets for fast implementation.
- **Version control:** Tracks changes to designs and enables comparison between different versions.
- **User and role management:** Manage team members and access rights.
- **Plugins and API:** Extensible through plugins and API access for custom workflows.

## Pros and cons

### Pros
- Easy transition from design to development through automated specifications.
- Improved communication and fewer misunderstandings within the team.
- Support for various design tools.
- Free entry with the freemium model.
- Clear and intuitive user interface.
- Fast delivery of assets and code snippets.

### Cons
- Some advanced features are only available in paid plans.
- Costs can rise for very large teams or projects.
- Depends on an internet connection, since it is a cloud-based platform.
- Limited options for editing designs directly in Zeplin.
- Automatic specifications may not always capture every design detail perfectly, which can require follow-up work.

## Pricing & costs

Zeplin offers a freemium model that allows you to get started at no cost. The free version is suitable for smaller teams or individuals and includes basic features as well as a limited number of projects. For larger teams or more extensive projects, there are various paid plans that offer additional features, more projects, expanded support, and more user accounts. Exact pricing varies depending on the provider and plan. A detailed overview can be found on Zeplin's official website.

## Alternatives to Zeplin

- **InVision:** Offers extensive prototyping and collaboration features for design and development teams.
- **Figma:** A web-based design and collaboration tool with comprehensive real-time collaboration features.
- **Avocode:** Enables design handoff with automatic specifications and asset export for developers.
- **Abstract:** A version control tool for design files, especially for Sketch, with collaboration features.
- **Marvel:** A simple prototyping and collaboration tool with additional features for user testing.

## FAQ

**1. What exactly is Zeplin?**  
Zeplin is a platform that connects design and development teams by turning design files into developer-friendly specifications and assets.

**2. Which design programs does Zeplin support?**  
Zeplin supports common design tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD, Figma, and Photoshop.

**3. Is there a free version of Zeplin?**  
Yes, Zeplin offers a freemium model with limited features and projects for individuals or small teams.

**4. How does Zeplin help developers?**  
Zeplin automatically provides measurements, colors, fonts, and code snippets so developers can implement designs faster and more accurately.

**5. Can you edit designs in Zeplin?**  
Zeplin is not a design tool for editing; it is used for handoff and communication around design projects.

**6. How does team collaboration work?**  
Team members can leave comments directly on the designs to coordinate feedback and changes.

**7. Is Zeplin cloud-based?**  
Yes, Zeplin is a cloud-based platform that can be used from anywhere with internet access.
