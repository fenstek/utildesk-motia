# BigBlueButton

BigBlueButton is an open-source web conferencing solution developed specifically for the education sector, but it can also be used in businesses and other organizations in many different ways. The platform enables interactive online meetings with video, audio, chat, a whiteboard, and screen sharing. Thanks to its open architecture, BigBlueButton can be flexibly adapted to individual needs and integrated into existing learning management systems (LMS).

## Who is BigBlueButton suitable for?

BigBlueButton is aimed primarily at educational institutions, trainers, and companies looking for a reliable, cost-effective solution for online classes, training, or meetings. It is suitable for:

- Schools, colleges, and universities that need virtual classrooms
- Trainers and coaches who want to run interactive webinars
- Companies that want to organize video conferences and remote meetings
- Developers and IT administrators who want to self-host and customize an open-source platform

Through its integration with many learning platforms, BigBlueButton is ideal for anyone who wants to promote online learning and collaboration.

## Key features

- Video and audio conferences with multiple participants
- Real-time chat for everyone or private messages
- Shared whiteboard for visualization and annotation
- Screen sharing for presentations and demonstrations
- Breakout rooms for group discussion
- Session recording for later playback
- Integration into various learning management systems (e.g. Moodle, Canvas)
- Multi-platform support (web browsers, mobile devices)
- Moderation tools such as participant management and raise-hand function
- Support for polls and voting

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Open source and free to use without licensing fees
- Specifically optimized for educational purposes with interactive features
- Flexible customization and integration into existing systems
- No dependence on commercial providers
- Active community and regular development
- Privacy-friendly thanks to self-hosting options

### Cons

- Technical setup and hosting require IT knowledge
- Performance depends on your own server and internet connection
- The user interface is functional, but less modern than some commercial alternatives
- Support is mainly provided through the community and documentation, with no official customer service
- Scaling can be challenging for very large numbers of participants

## Pricing & Costs

BigBlueButton is open source and can be self-hosted free of charge. For users without their own infrastructure, some providers offer hosted solutions that are subject to a fee depending on performance and scope. Prices vary depending on the provider, plan, and number of users.

## Alternatives to BigBlueButton

- **Zoom** – Widely used video conferencing platform with extensive features, paid plans with a freemium model.
- **Jitsi Meet** – Open-source video conferencing solution that is easy to use and also free.
- **Microsoft Teams** – Integrated collaboration platform with video conferencing, subscription-based.
- **Google Meet** – Video conferencing with strong integration into Google Workspace, freemium model.
- **Adobe Connect** – Powerful webinar and meeting platform, paid with a subscription.

## FAQ

**1. Is BigBlueButton free?**  
Yes, BigBlueButton is open-source software and can be used free of charge if you host it yourself.

**2. Do I need technical knowledge to use BigBlueButton?**  
No special knowledge is required to use it as a participant. However, IT knowledge is recommended for installation and hosting.

**3. Can I integrate BigBlueButton into my learning management system?**  
Yes, BigBlueButton offers integrations for many LMSs such as Moodle, Canvas, or Sakai.

**4. How secure is BigBlueButton?**  
Because it can be self-hosted, security is largely in the hands of the operator. The software supports encryption and data protection requirements.

**5. How many participants can join a BigBlueButton session?**  
The maximum number of participants depends on server capacity and bandwidth. Typically, up to 100 participants are possible.

**6. Is there a way to record sessions?**  
Yes, BigBlueButton supports recording meetings and webinars.

**7. Which devices are supported?**  
BigBlueButton runs in modern web browsers and is compatible with Windows, macOS, Linux, and mobile devices.

**8. Is there official support for BigBlueButton?**  
Main support is provided through the community, documentation, and forums. Some providers offer commercial support for hosted solutions.
