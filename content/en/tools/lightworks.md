# Lightworks

Lightworks is a professional video editing software that stands out for its powerful editing features and user-friendly interface. It is used by both ambitious filmmakers and experienced editors to create high-quality video projects. The software supports a wide range of formats and offers extensive tools for editing, color correction, and post-production.

## Who is Lightworks suitable for?

Lightworks is especially well suited for professional filmmakers, video producers, and content creators who are looking for a flexible and powerful solution for video editing. Beginners with some video editing experience can also benefit from its intuitive operation. The software is ideal for projects ranging from short clips to full-length feature films. Thanks to its extensive tools, Lightworks is suitable for both post-production and finishing.

## Key features

- Non-linear video editing with precise timeline editing
- Support for numerous video formats and codecs
- Real-time effects and transitions for professional-looking results
- Color correction and grading tools
- Multicam editing for parallel camera recordings
- Export options in various resolutions up to 4K (depending on the plan)
- Integration of titles and subtitles
- Support for VST audio effects
- Collaboration features for team projects (depending on the plan)
- Automatic backup and project management

## Pros and cons

### Pros

- Extensive and professional editing tools
- Supports a wide range of video formats
- Real-time preview for fast editing results
- Multicam editing makes complex projects easier
- Available across platforms (Windows, macOS, Linux)

### Cons

- The interface can initially feel complex for beginners
- Resource-intensive, so powerful computers are recommended
- No built-in cloud storage, external solutions are required

## Pricing & costs

Lightworks pricing varies depending on the provider and the plan chosen. There is a free version with limited features that is sufficient for simple projects. For professional users, various subscriptions are available that include additional features such as 4K export, advanced effects, and collaboration options. Exact costs and licensing models can be found on the official website or through authorized retailers.
