# FollowUpThen

FollowUpThen is a practical tool for automating email reminders, particularly suited for managing follow-ups and tasks in the workplace. It can be easily integrated into existing email systems and helps users keep track of important messages and deadlines without having to open additional apps.

## For whom is FollowUpThen suitable?

FollowUpThen is primarily aimed at professionals who work extensively with email communication and want to boost their productivity by automating reminders. It is particularly useful for:

- Sales representatives who don't want to forget follow-ups with customers.
- Project managers who need to keep track of deadlines and tasks.
- Small and medium-sized enterprises that want to use simple CRM features without a lot of hassle.
- Anyone who wants to structure their email communication more efficiently.

## Key Features

- **Email-based Reminders:** Users send an email to a special address (e.g., 1week@followupthen.com) to be reminded at a later time.
- **Flexible Time Scheduling:** Reminders can be set for minutes, hours, days, weeks, or months.
- **Recurring Follow-ups:** Automated reminders are repeated at defined intervals.
- **Integration with Email Clients:** Compatible with all major email providers and requires no additional software installation.
- **Additional Notes:** Reminders can be accompanied by notes or context information.
- **Team and Group Functions:** Shared use of reminders in teams (depending on the plan).
- **CRM-like Features:** Simple tracking of contacts and follow-up status.
- **Customizable Response Templates:** Automated responses to follow-up emails.

## Advantages and Disadvantages

### Advantages

- Very easy to use without additional software.
- Compatible with all email programs.
- Saves time with automated reminders and follow-ups.
- Suitable for individuals and small teams.
- Flexible adjustment of reminder intervals.
- Helps prevent important tasks and customer contacts from being forgotten.

### Disadvantages

- Limited functionality compared to comprehensive CRM or project management tools.
- The email-based interface may be unfamiliar to some users.
- Advanced team functions and automations are usually only available in paid plans.
- No native mobile app; usage occurs through the email client on mobile devices.
- Privacy and security concerns regarding the processing of emails should be considered.

## FAQ

**1. How does FollowUpThen work?**  
FollowUpThen works through email addresses, where users send messages with a time specification (e.g., 2days@followupthen.com) to be reminded at a later time.

**2. Do I need special software?**  
No, the tool works completely through email and requires no additional installation.

**3. Can I use FollowUpThen in a team?**  
Yes, team functions are available depending on the chosen plan, allowing shared management of reminders.

**4. Is FollowUpThen secure?**  
The tool processes emails, so users should be aware of data protection and secure communication. Details can be found in the provider's privacy policy.

**5. Is there a free version?**  
Yes, FollowUpThen offers a free basic version with limited reminder functions.

**6. Can I connect FollowUpThen with my CRM?**  
Limited integrations are available, but the tool can be used as a simple addition to CRM communication.

**7. How flexible are the reminder times?**  
Reminders can be set for different time intervals such as minutes, hours, days, or weeks.

**8. Is there a mobile app?**  
FollowUpThen has no native app; usage occurs through the email client on mobile devices.
