# LiveAgent

LiveAgent is a comprehensive customer service software that helps companies manage communication with customers efficiently. By integrating different communication channels such as email, live chat, phone, and social media, LiveAgent provides a central platform for customer support. The solution uses AI-powered features to process requests faster and improve the customer experience.

## Who is LiveAgent suitable for?

LiveAgent is aimed at small and medium-sized businesses as well as larger organizations that want to optimize their customer service. The software is particularly suitable for companies that want to manage multiple communication channels at the same time and are looking for a centralized solution for support, CRM, and communication. Across industries, teams that value fast response times and structured handling of customer inquiries benefit most.

## Key Features

- **Multichannel ticket system:** Manage customer inquiries from email, live chat, phone, social networks, and more in a single interface.
- **Live chat and chatbot:** Real-time communication with customers, including AI-based chatbots for automating routine questions.
- **Automation and workflows:** Automatic ticket assignment, prioritization, and escalation rules to increase efficiency.
- **Knowledge base:** Create and manage FAQs and help documents for customers and support staff.
- **Reporting and analytics:** Evaluate support performance, customer satisfaction, and team productivity.
- **CRM integration:** Manage customer contacts and interactions for better customer retention.
- **Multilingual support:** Support for different languages for international customer service.
- **Mobile apps:** Access support systems on iOS and Android devices.
