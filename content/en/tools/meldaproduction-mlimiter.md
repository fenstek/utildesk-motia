# MeldaProduction MLimiter

MeldaProduction MLimiter is a powerful and versatile limiter plugin designed specifically for audio mastering. It helps push audio signals to maximum loudness without distortion or loss of quality. The tool offers an intuitive interface and a wide range of features for precisely controlling dynamics and professionally refining the sound. MLimiter is free and suitable for both beginners and experienced producers.

## Who is MeldaProduction MLimiter suitable for?

MLimiter is aimed at musicians, producers, audio engineers, and anyone working in music production. The plugin is especially useful for those who want to bring their tracks up to a professional loudness level during the mastering process without affecting sound quality. Podcasters and sound designers who need reliable dynamic control also benefit from the limiter's features. Because it is free, it is ideal for users with a limited budget or for those who want to try different tools.

## Key features

- Precise loudness limiting without audible distortion

- Compatibility with all common DAWs and platforms
