# Innography

Innography is a patent analytics platform for teams that need more than a basic patent search. It helps IP departments, researchers and innovation managers turn patent data into a clearer view of competitors, technology areas and portfolio risk.

Use it when patent research is part of a larger strategic workflow: market mapping, R&D planning, freedom-to-operate checks or portfolio reviews.
