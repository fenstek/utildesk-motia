# Mattermost

Mattermost is an open-source platform for team communication and collaboration that stands out for its adaptability and automation capabilities. In the area of artificial intelligence (AI) in particular, Mattermost offers features that help teams organize their workflows more efficiently and automate repetitive tasks. The platform combines chat, workflow automation, and AI-powered assistance in a secure environment that is suitable for both small teams and large enterprises.

## Who is Mattermost suitable for?

Mattermost is aimed at companies and organizations that are looking for a flexible, secure, and customizable communication solution. Mattermost is especially suitable for:

- IT and development teams that prefer open-source solutions and want to make their own customizations.
- Companies that value privacy and control over their data, since Mattermost can be self-hosted.
- Teams that want to improve their workflows through AI-powered automation and assistance.
- Organizations looking for an alternative to proprietary chat and collaboration tools while valuing workflow integration.
- Users who need a platform that can be easily integrated into existing systems and tools.

## Key Features

- **Team chat and messaging:** Real-time communication with channels, direct messages, and threads.
- **AI-powered assistance:** Integration of AI tools to support tasks and decision-making.
- **Workflow automation:** Creation and management of automated processes to improve efficiency.
- **Open-source architecture:** Ability to customize and extend individually.
- **Self-hosting:** Full control over data by hosting on your own servers.
- **Integrations:** Support for numerous plugins and interfaces to other applications.
- **Security features:** Encryption, compliance options, and user management.
- **Mobile and desktop apps:** Cross-platform use on different devices.
- **Custom bots:** Development and deployment of bots for automation and support.
- **Scalability:** Suitable for small teams through to large enterprises.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- High flexibility through open source and self-hosting.
- A wide range of automation and AI features.
- Strong security and privacy options.
- Extensive integration options.
- Cross-platform availability.
- Community and enterprise support available.

### Cons

- Setup and administration can require technical expertise.
- For smaller teams, the variety of features can feel overwhelming.
- Some advanced features are only included in paid plans.
- The user interface is less modern compared with some competitors.

## Pricing & Costs

Mattermost offers a freemium model with a free basic version that includes many core features. Different subscription plans are available for advanced features, support, and enterprise requirements. Prices vary depending on the plan and number of users. There are also custom quotes for large customers and special requirements.

## Alternatives to Mattermost

- **Slack:** Widely used team chat platform with many integrations, paid plans.
- **Microsoft Teams:** Integrated collaboration solution with Office 365 connection, subscription-based.
- **Rocket.Chat:** Open-source alternative with self-hosting and similar features.
- **Discord:** Originally for gamers, now also used for team communication, freemium model.
- **Zulip:** Team chat with thread-oriented communication, open source and cloud options.

## FAQ

**1. Is Mattermost free to use?**  
Yes, Mattermost offers a free basic version with limited features. Paid subscriptions are required for advanced features.

**2. Can Mattermost be self-hosted?**  
Yes, Mattermost is open source and can be installed on your own servers, giving you full control over the data.

**3. What AI features does Mattermost offer?**  
Mattermost supports AI-powered assistance and automation, for example through bots and workflow integration, which can vary depending on the plan and integration.

**4. How secure is Mattermost?**  
Mattermost offers comprehensive security features such as encryption, compliance options, and user permission management, ideal for companies with high privacy requirements.

**5. Which platforms is Mattermost available on?**  
Mattermost is available as a web application as well as a desktop and mobile app for various operating systems.

**6. Which integrations does Mattermost support?**  
Mattermost can be integrated with numerous tools and services, including DevOps, project management, and AI tools, depending on your needs and plan.

**7. How does Mattermost differ from Slack?**  
Mattermost is open source and can be self-hosted, while Slack is a proprietary cloud solution. Mattermost offers more customization options and control over data.

**8. Is support available for Mattermost?**  
Support is available in a limited form in the freemium model. For enterprises, there are paid plans with professional support and an SLA.
