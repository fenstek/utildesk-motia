# Play.ht

Play.ht is an advanced text-to-speech platform that makes it possible to turn written content into natural, realistic audio recordings. The solution is aimed at developers, content creators, and businesses that need high-quality speech synthesis for podcasts, audiobooks, e-learning, and other applications. With a wide selection of voices and languages, as well as a user-friendly API, Play.ht offers flexible ways to integrate audio content.

## Who is Play.ht suitable for?

Play.ht is especially suitable for:

- **Content creators** and bloggers who want to offer their articles as audio.
- **Developers and businesses** that want to integrate speech synthesis into their apps, websites, or products.
- **Education providers** and e-learning platforms that want to present learning content in audio form.
- **Marketing and media agencies** that want to create podcasts or commercials with natural voices.
- Anyone looking for a simple and scalable text-to-speech solution without having to produce their own voice recordings.

## Key features

- **Realistic AI voices:** Large selection of natural voices in many languages and dialects.
- **API access:** Easy integration of TTS technology into your own applications via a powerful REST API.
- **Text-to-audio conversion:** Fast conversion of text into MP3 or WAV files.
- **Customizable voice settings:** Control over pitch, speaking speed, and pauses.
- **Cloud-based storage:** Access and manage created audio files online.
- **Multiple languages and accents:** Support for international content.
- **Batch processing:** Conversion of multiple texts at once is possible.
- **SSML support:** Advanced control over speech output through Speech Synthesis Markup Language.
- **Download and sharing options:** Easily download or share audio files.
- **Security and privacy:** Storage and processing of data according to the provider's policy.
