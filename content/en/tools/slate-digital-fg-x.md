# Slate Digital FG-X

Slate Digital FG-X is a professional mastering tool developed specifically for music production. It combines powerful limiter and compressor algorithms to maximize a track's loudness without compromising sound quality. FG-X is known for its ability to keep the sound transparent while maintaining a mix that feels dynamic and alive.

Thanks to its intuitive interface, FG-X is also accessible to hobby musicians who want to prepare their tracks professionally on their own.

- **Customizable presets:** Offers a wide range of presets for different music styles and starting points.
