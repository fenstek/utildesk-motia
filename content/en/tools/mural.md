# MURAL

MURAL is a digital collaboration platform designed specifically for visual brainstorming, teamwork, and creative processes. It provides a virtual whiteboard environment where teams can develop, organize, and present ideas together, regardless of location. With a wide range of features, MURAL supports innovative ways of working and encourages real-time exchange.

## Who is MURAL suitable for?

MURAL is aimed at teams and companies of all sizes that value creative collaboration. The tool is especially useful for:

- Project managers and teams using agile methods such as Design Thinking or Scrum
- Creative agencies and marketing departments that want to develop ideas visually
- Educational institutions and trainers running interactive workshops
- Remote teams looking for a central platform for collaboration and brainstorming

The intuitive interface makes MURAL accessible to both experienced users and beginners.

## Main features

- **Virtual whiteboard:** Unlimited workspace for notes, drawings, diagrams, and more
- **Real-time collaboration:** Multiple users can work on boards at the same time and see changes live
- **Template library:** Numerous ready-made templates for brainstorming, retrospectives, customer journey maps, and more
- **Commenting:** Feedback and discussions directly on the content
- **Timer and voting tools:** Support for structured workshops and decision-making processes
- **Integration:** Connects with common tools such as Microsoft Teams, Slack, Jira, and others
- **Security features:** Data encryption and access controls for protected collaboration

## Pros and cons

### Pros

- Intuitive user interface that enables quick onboarding
- Diverse features specifically for creative and agile ways of working
- Supports collaboration regardless of time and place
- Extensive templates make it easier to get started with different methods
- Good integration into existing work environments

### Cons

- The large number of features can be overwhelming for beginners at first
- Depending on the selected plan, some features may be limited
- For very large teams or complex projects, costs can be higher
- Offline use is only possible to a limited extent

## Pricing & costs

MURAL offers different pricing plans based on feature scope and number of users. Typically, these include:

- **Free basic version:** With limited features and a restricted number of boards
- **Team and Business plans:** Expanded features such as unlimited boards, advanced integrations, and support
- **Enterprise solutions:** Customized offers with additional security and administration options

Exact prices vary depending on the provider and contract terms. It is recommended to consult the official website for up-to-date information.

## Alternatives to MURAL

- **Miro:** Also a popular online whiteboard that offers similar features for team collaboration
- **Microsoft Whiteboard:** Part of the Microsoft 365 suite, ideal for users already working in the Microsoft ecosystem
- **Conceptboard:** Focuses on visual collaboration with extensive commenting features
- **Jamboard:** Google’s digital whiteboard, well integrated into Google Workspace
- **Stormboard:** Offers brainstorming and project management tools with a focus on ease of use
