# Weglot

Weglot is a powerful tool for multilingual website translation and localization. It allows companies to offer their online presence quickly and efficiently in multiple languages without needing extensive technical skills. Combining automatic translation with manual editing options, Weglot helps both small and large businesses reach international audiences and improve visibility in search engines.

## Who is Weglot suitable for?

Weglot is ideal for:

- Companies and agencies that want to make their websites multilingual without building their own translation department.
- E-commerce operators aiming to attract international customers and present their products in different languages.
- Marketing teams that prioritize SEO-optimized translations to improve global search rankings.
- Developers and website owners seeking easy integration with popular CMS platforms such as WordPress, Shopify, Wix, and others.
- Startups and SMEs looking to achieve professional localization with a limited budget.

## Key Features

- **Automatic translation:** Fast machine translation of website content into over 100 languages.
- **Manual editing:** Ability to adjust and optimize translations directly within the dashboard.
- **SEO-friendly translations:** Automatic adjustment of meta tags, URLs, and hreflang tags for better search engine ranking.
- **Seamless integration:** Compatible with numerous CMS and e-commerce platforms.
- **Language switcher:** User-friendly button to select the language on the website.
- **Translation management:** Collaboration features for teams to edit translations together.
- **Automatic detection of new content:** Newly added or changed content is automatically detected and translated.
- **Support for dynamic content:** Translates content loaded via JavaScript or APIs.
- **Performance optimization:** Fast loading times despite multilingual content through optimized technical implementation.

## Advantages and Disadvantages

### Advantages

- Easy and quick setup without programming knowledge.
- Wide language support with high translation quality.
- SEO-optimized translations increase international visibility.
- Flexible pricing model with various subscription plans.
- Good integration with many popular web platforms.
- Options for manual correction and adjustment of translations.
- Automatic detection and translation of new content saves time.

### Disadvantages

- Costs can increase with the number of pages and language versions.
- Automatic translation may have limitations on highly customized websites.
- Requires an internet connection to use the cloud-based solution.
- Some features are available only in higher-tier plans.
- Manual post-editing can be time-consuming for large websites.

## Pricing & Costs

Weglot offers a subscription model with different plans based on the number of words to be translated and languages supported. Generally, there is a free trial phase or a freemium plan with limited functionality. Prices increase depending on website size and desired service level. Customized offers can also be requested for specific needs.

Typical price components:

- Number of translatable words (limited according to plan).
- Number of supported languages.
- Additional features such as team access or extended support.

## Alternatives to Weglot

- **Google Translate Website Translator:** Free machine translation with simple integration but without SEO optimization.
- **Bablic:** Translation platform focused on visual editing and customization, also subscription-based.
- **Localize:** Professional localization solution with extensive translation management features.
- **TranslatePress:** WordPress plugin allowing front-end translation with various pricing models.
- **GTranslate:** Automatic website translation with SEO integration and different pricing options.

## FAQ

**1. How does Weglot integrate into my website?**  
Weglot offers plugins and simple script integrations for many CMS and platforms. After registration and setup, a language switcher automatically appears on the website.

**2. Is Weglot's translation SEO-friendly?**  
Yes, Weglot creates translated pages with their own URLs, meta tags, and hreflang attributes, helping search engines correctly index multilingual content.

**3. Can I manually edit automatic translations?**  
Yes, translations can be edited, corrected, and supplemented by professional translators directly in the Weglot dashboard.

**4. Which languages does Weglot support?**  
Weglot supports over 100 languages, including both common and less widely spoken languages.

**5. Is there a free version of Weglot?**  
Weglot offers a freemium plan with limited features and word count, ideal for testing or small websites.

**6. How is billing handled at Weglot?**  
Billing is subscription-based, typically monthly or yearly, depending on the chosen plan.

**7. Is Weglot suitable for e-commerce websites?**  
Yes, Weglot is compatible with platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce and supports translation of product pages and checkout processes.

**8. How secure is my data with Weglot?**  
Weglot places great importance on data protection and uses secure servers. Detailed information depends on the provider's specific privacy policies.
