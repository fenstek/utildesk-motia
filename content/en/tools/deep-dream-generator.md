# Deep Dream Generator

The Deep Dream Generator is a machine learning-based tool that transforms images and videos into creative, surreal visualizations through automated image processing. Users can create unique designs that are particularly suitable for creative projects, marketing, or social media.

## Who is Deep Dream Generator for?

Deep Dream Generator is designed for designers, artists, content creators, and anyone who wants to experiment with creative image and video editing using artificial intelligence. Marketing professionals and social media managers also benefit from the ability to quickly create attention-grabbing visual content. Due to its intuitive interface, the tool is suitable for both beginners and experienced users who want to boost their productivity in image editing.

## Key Features

- **AI-based Image Processing:** Automatic transformation of images into artistic, dreamlike representations.
- **Video Support:** Ability to apply similar effects to video material.
- **Various Style Templates:** Selection of different style templates to create different artistic effects.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Easy to use even without technical knowledge.
- **Cloud-Based:** Processing takes place online, no local processing required.
- **Community Features:** Sharing and discovery of creations by other users.
- **Automation Potential:** Quick implementation of creative ideas without manual design.
- **Export Options:** Download of created images and videos in various formats.
