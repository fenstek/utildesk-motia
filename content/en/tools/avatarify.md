# Avatarify

With Avatarify, it is worth taking a sober look at the everyday reality behind the promise. At its core, it focuses on face animation and avatar effects for video experiments; the tool becomes truly valuable when it helps create visual prototypes, filters, and playful live formats.

Before introducing it, the key question should be answered: are rights, labeling, and context clearly defined? Otherwise, the benefit remains difficult to measure. The most important caution is that it becomes problematic and misleading when used on real people without consent.

## Who is Avatarify suitable for?

Avatarify is a good option for organizations where face animation and avatar effects for video experiments regularly take time. Getting started is especially worthwhile for creative experiments, demos, and non-critical video formats. A clearly assigned owner should accompany the process.

The tool is not ideal when the caution point remains hard to control: it becomes problematic and misleading when used on real people without consent. In that case, the process should be simplified first before additional software is introduced.
