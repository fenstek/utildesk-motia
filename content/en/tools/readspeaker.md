# ReadSpeaker

ReadSpeaker is an innovative text-to-speech solution that makes it possible to convert digital content into naturally sounding speech. The technology helps companies, educational institutions, and developers create accessible and interactive applications. ReadSpeaker offers a wide range of voices and languages to improve accessibility and the user experience.

## Who is ReadSpeaker suitable for?

ReadSpeaker is aimed at companies, educational institutions, publishers, and developers who want to make their digital content more accessible. The tool is especially useful for:

- Website owners who want to make their content accessible to people with visual impairments or reading difficulties.
- E-learning platforms that want to supplement multimedia learning content with audio.
- Publishers and media companies that want to offer articles or books in audio form.
- Developers who want to integrate text-to-speech functionality into their own applications.

## Main features

- **Text-to-speech conversion:** Converts text into natural speech in real time.
- **Wide selection of languages and voices:** Supports many languages and different voices, including regional accents.
- **Integration into websites and apps:** Easy implementation via API or widgets.
- **Accessibility:** Supports WCAG standards to improve accessibility.
- **Customizable audio parameters:** Control over speech rate, pitch, and volume.
- **Offline and online use:** Depending on the plan and use case.
- **Multi-platform support:** Compatible with desktop, mobile devices, and tablets.
- **Analytics and user statistics:** Insight into usage and interaction with the voice output.
