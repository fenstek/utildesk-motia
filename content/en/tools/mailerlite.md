# MailerLite

MailerLite is a user-friendly email marketing platform that helps businesses and individuals create and send professional newsletters and automated campaigns. With a clean interface and a wide range of features, MailerLite is especially well suited for small to mid-sized businesses that want to manage their marketing communication simply and effectively.

## Who is MailerLite for?

MailerLite is primarily aimed at small and medium-sized businesses, freelancers, bloggers, and marketing teams looking for a cost-effective solution for email marketing. Users without deep technical knowledge also benefit from the intuitive interface and ready-made templates. With automation features and segmentation, MailerLite is also interesting for users who want to optimize their marketing processes and send personalized content.

## Key Features

- Drag-and-drop email editor for easy newsletter design  
- Automation of email campaigns with workflows  
- Creation and management of subscriber lists and segments  
- Responsive templates for mobile and desktop devices  
- A/B testing to optimize subject lines and content  
- Integration with other tools and platforms (e.g. Shopify, WordPress)  
- Pop-ups and signup forms for lead generation  
- Detailed reports and analytics features for campaign performance  
- Multilingual user interface and support  
- GDPR-compliant data management and opt-in processes  

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- Simple and intuitive user interface  
- Extensive automation features even in the free plan  
- Good integration with numerous third-party apps  
- Responsive designs and modern templates  
- Freemium model allows getting started at no cost  
- Ongoing development and support  

### Cons
- Some advanced features are only available in paid plans  
- Limited customization options for certain templates  
- Larger companies with complex requirements may run into limitations  
- Support is sometimes available only in English  

## Pricing & Costs

MailerLite offers a freemium pricing model. The free version allows sending emails to a limited number of subscribers with basic features. For advanced features such as unlimited emails, additional automations, priority support, and advanced integrations, various subscription plans are available. Prices vary depending on the number of subscribers and the selected plan.

## FAQ

**1. Is MailerLite free to use?**  
Yes, MailerLite offers a free plan with limited subscribers and features, which is sufficient for many small users.

**2. What automation features are available?**  
MailerLite allows you to create automated email workflows, such as welcome series, birthday messages, or follow-ups based on user actions.

**3. How many emails can I send with the free plan?**  
The free plan limits the number of monthly emails; the exact number depends on the current offer.

**4. Can I divide my subscribers into groups or segments?**  
Yes, MailerLite supports subscriber segmentation to create targeted campaigns.

**5. Is MailerLite GDPR-compliant?**  
MailerLite offers features for GDPR compliance, including opt-in forms and data management.

**6. Which integrations are possible?**  
MailerLite can be connected with many platforms, including e-commerce systems, CMSs, and web apps.

**7. Is there a mobile app for management?**  
MailerLite offers a mobile app with limited features for managing campaigns and subscribers.

**8. How can I contact support?**  
Support is available by email or live chat depending on the plan, with priority for paying customers.
