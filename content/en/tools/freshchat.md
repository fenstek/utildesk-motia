# Freshchat

Freshchat is a modern messaging software that helps businesses communicate with customers in real time across multiple channels. By using AI-powered chatbots and automated workflows, Freshchat improves customer support, increases efficiency, and enables personalized interactions. The platform is especially well suited for customer service, sales, and marketing.

## Who is Freshchat for?

Freshchat is designed for businesses of all sizes that want to digitize and optimize their customer communication. The tool is particularly suitable for:

- Customer service teams that want to handle requests quickly and efficiently.
- Sales teams that want to qualify and nurture leads automatically.
- Marketing teams that want to retain customers with personalized messages.
- E-commerce companies that want to support their online store with live chat and chatbots.
- Organizations that follow an omnichannel approach and want to communicate across different channels.

## Main Features

- **Multi-channel messaging:** Integration of chat on websites, mobile apps, social media, and messaging platforms.
- **AI chatbots:** Automated responses to customer inquiries around the clock.
- **Workflow automation:** Creation of automated processes for lead qualification and ticket routing.
- **Team inbox:** Shared management of customer inquiries with assignment and collaboration.
- **Personalized messages:** Segmentation of users and delivery of targeted campaigns.
- **Analytics & reporting:** Evaluation of chat performance and customer satisfaction.
- **Integration with CRM & other tools:** Connection to common systems for seamless data transfer.
- **Mobile apps:** Access to chats and management on the go.
- **Security features:** Privacy-compliant communication and user management.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Support for multiple channels for centralized customer communication.
- Flexible workflow automation to optimize processes.
- Extensive integration options with other business applications.

### Cons

- Costs can vary depending on the feature set and number of users and are not always transparent.

- Limited customization options in the basic plan.

## Pricing & Costs

Freshchat offers different pricing plans based on the feature set and number of users. Typically, there are:

- A free starter plan with basic messaging features.
- Paid plans with advanced features such as AI chatbots, automations, and integrations.
- Prices may be billed monthly or annually and vary depending on the provider and selected plan.

For exact pricing details, it is recommended to check the official website, as these may be adjusted depending on region and offer.
