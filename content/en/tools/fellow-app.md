# Fellow.app

Fellow.app is an intelligent meeting management platform designed to boost the productivity of teams and improve collaboration. With a focus on efficient meetings, Fellow.app helps teams prepare agendas, take notes, and define clear action points. The application uses smart features to make meetings more transparent and goal-oriented.

## For whom is Fellow.app suitable?

Fellow.app is particularly suitable for teams and organizations that regularly hold meetings and want to optimize the process and results of these meetings. It is ideal for project managers, team leaders, HR personnel, and anyone who values structured communication and clear task allocation. Remote working teams also benefit from the collaborative features to work effectively despite physical distance.

## Key Features

- **Create and share meeting agendas:** Users can create agendas in advance and share them with participants to ease preparation.
- **Real-time note-taking:** Notes can be taken directly during the meeting, making them accessible to all participants.
- **Task and action point assignment:** Define and assign tasks with status tracking to clarify responsibilities.
- **Feedback and 1:1 meeting tools:** Support for regular feedback sessions and individual meetings with templates.
- **Calendar integration:** Synchronization with popular calendar services like Google Calendar and Outlook.
- **Reminders and follow-ups:** Automatic reminders for open tasks and next steps.
- **Analytics and reports:** Insights into meeting efficiency and team performance (depending on the plan).
- **Mobile and desktop apps:** Access from various devices for maximum flexibility.

## Advantages and Disadvantages

## Pricing & Costs

Fellow.app offers a **Freemium** pricing model. The basic version is free to use and includes fundamental meeting and collaboration features. For enhanced features like detailed reports, expanded integrations, and additional team management options, there are paid subscriptions. Prices vary depending on company size and functional scope.
