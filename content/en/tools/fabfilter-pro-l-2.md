## Key Features

- **Transparent signal limiting:** Prevents overload and clipping at maximum loudness.
- **Multiple limiter modes:** Different algorithms for different sonic characteristics and applications.
- **Loudness metering:** Built-in tools for monitoring loudness according to industry standards (LUFS, True Peak).
- **Oversampling:** Up to 8x oversampling for reduced distortion and higher sound quality.
- **Adaptive release function:** Dynamic adjustment of release time for musical and natural behavior.
- **Lookahead function:** Allows precise intervention in the signal to control peaks cleanly.
- **Stereo and multichannel support:** Compatible with stereo and surround formats.
- **Fully customizable user interface:** Scalable, with different display modes and real-time visualizations.
- **Automation and DAW integration:** Supports all common DAWs with extensive automation capabilities.
- **Presets:** An extensive collection of preset parameters for different music styles and applications.
