# Sendible

Sendible is a comprehensive social media management platform designed specifically for businesses, agencies, and marketing teams. With a focus on automation and AI-powered features, Sendible helps you plan, publish, and analyze social media content efficiently. The platform supports numerous social networks and enables centralized control of all marketing activities.

## Who is Sendible suitable for?

Sendible is primarily aimed at small to medium-sized businesses, social media agencies, and marketing teams that manage multiple channels at the same time. The tool is especially well suited for users who value simple content planning, team collaboration, and detailed reports. Freelancers and influencers who want to manage their social media presence professionally will also find Sendible a suitable solution.

## Key features

- **Multi-channel management:** Manage multiple social media accounts such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and more in one place.
- **Content planning and scheduling:** Create and schedule posts in advance to maintain a consistent presence.
- **AI-powered content suggestions:** Use smart recommendations for relevant content and topics.
- **Team collaboration:** Work with multiple users, assign tasks, and manage approval processes.
- **Social listening:** Monitor brand and industry conversations to spot trends and customer opinions early.
- **Analytics and reporting:** Get detailed reports on the performance of your posts and campaigns.
- **Third-party integrations:** Connect Sendible with additional marketing tools and CRM systems.
- **Mobile app:** Manage your social media activities on the go through the app.
- **Automation:** Use recurring tasks and automatic publishing.
- **Inbox management:** Centralize messages and comments from different networks.
