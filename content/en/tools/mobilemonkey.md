# Customers.ai (formerly MobileMonkey)

Customers.ai, formerly known as MobileMonkey, is an AI-powered platform for marketing automation and customer communication across multiple channels. The platform enables businesses to create and manage chatbots for Facebook Messenger, Instagram, SMS, and web chat to generate leads, support customers, and increase sales. With the rebrand to Customers.ai, the focus was strengthened on AI-based customer interactions and personalized conversation management.

## Who is Customers.ai for?

Customers.ai is aimed at marketing teams, e-commerce businesses, and small to medium-sized companies that want to automate their customer communication and keep it consistent across different channels. The platform is especially suitable for:

- Marketing managers who want to automate lead generation and nurturing
- E-commerce stores that want to handle customer service and sales processes through chatbots
- Social media managers who use Facebook Messenger and Instagram for customer communication
- Companies that want to implement multichannel marketing automation (Messenger, SMS, email, web chat)
- Agencies that provide chatbot solutions for clients

## FAQ

**1. Why was MobileMonkey renamed to Customers.ai?**
The rebrand reflects the expanded focus on AI-powered customer interactions and the platform's evolution beyond pure Messenger bots.

**2. Can I use Customers.ai for free?**
Yes, there is a free version with basic features for up to 1,000 contacts. Paid plans are required for advanced features and higher contact volumes.

**3. Which channels does Customers.ai support?**
The platform supports Facebook Messenger, Instagram, SMS, WhatsApp, email, and web chat.

**4. Do I need programming knowledge to create chatbots?**
No, Customers.ai offers a visual drag-and-drop builder that lets you create chatbots without code. Technical knowledge is helpful for more complex customizations.

**5. Can I integrate Customers.ai with my CRM or e-commerce system?**
Yes, the platform offers integrations with common tools such as HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, Zapier, and many more.

**6. Is Customers.ai suitable for small businesses too?**
Yes, thanks to the free version and scalable pricing models, the platform is also suitable for small businesses and startups.

**7. How does Customers.ai differ from other chatbot platforms?**
The focus is on multichannel marketing automation with a strong emphasis on Messenger platforms and AI-powered personalization.

**8. Can I also handle customer service with Customers.ai?**
Yes, the platform is suitable for both marketing automation and automated customer service and support.
