# Clarivate Analytics (Web of Science)

Clarivate Analytics is best known for Web of Science, a research and citation platform used by universities, companies and analysts. It helps users discover scientific literature, follow citation networks and evaluate research output.

Use it when research quality, provenance and citation context matter more than a simple web search.
