# QDA Miner

QDA Miner is a powerful qualitative data analysis software that helps researchers and analysts systematically code, analyze, and visualize large text datasets. The application is particularly well suited for analyzing interviews, surveys, focus groups, and other qualitative data sources. With a wide range of features, QDA Miner helps identify patterns and relationships in data and gain well-founded insights.

## Who is QDA Miner suitable for?

QDA Miner is primarily aimed at academics, market researchers, social scientists, and analysts who use qualitative and mixed methods. The software is suitable for both academic users and companies that want to analyze qualitative data. Consultants, journalists, and other professionals who need structured analysis of large volumes of text also benefit from its extensive features.

## Key Features

- **Coding of text data:** Allows you to mark and tag text passages for systematic analysis.
- **Visual analysis tools:** Provides charts, word clouds, cluster analyses, and other visualizations.
- **Statistical analysis:** Built-in functions for quantitative analysis of qualitative data.
- **Multimedia data integration:** Analyze audio, video, and image data alongside text.
- **Flexible data imports:** Supports various formats, e.g. Word, PDF, Excel, SPSS.
- **Team collaboration:** Features for working together and jointly editing projects.
- **Report generation:** Export options for reports and presentations.
- **Automated text mining tools:** Support for identifying topics and patterns.
