# Hypic

Hypic is a mobile image-editing app for quick retouching, looks, filters, social visuals, and AI-assisted image improvement.

Its appeal is speed: instead of a desktop Photoshop workflow, it focuses on instant smartphone editing, especially for creators, social media, and personal visual styles.

## Who is it for?

Hypic fits mobile creators, social teams, and users who want to improve images quickly. For professional retouching, print files, or deep layer control, Photoshop-style desktop tools are still more suitable.

## Typical use cases

- Edit portraits and social images directly on a phone
- Prepare quick looks, filters, and retouching for posts
- Test image ideas without a heavy desktop workflow
- Speed up creator content with a consistent look

## Core features

- Mobile image editing and retouching
- AI-assisted enhancement and style features
- Filters, looks, and social-ready outputs
- Low barrier to entry for fast results

## Pros and cons

### Pros

- Very fast for mobile editing
- Good fit for creators and social content
- Easy to start without a professional setup

### Cons

- Not suited to every professional production workflow
- App and platform rules can change
- Fine control is more limited than in desktop software

## Workflow fit

Hypic is a fast mobile tool, not a production system. That is exactly why it can be useful when images need to move from phone to content workflow without friction.

## Privacy & data notes

Photo apps can involve image content, faces, metadata, and cloud processing. Review privacy and storage rules before using sensitive client images.
