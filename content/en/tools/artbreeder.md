# Artbreeder

Artbreeder is an innovative online platform that utilizes artificial intelligence to create and modify creative image compositions. Users can create unique artworks by combining and adjusting images without requiring extensive design knowledge. The interface is intuitive, using sliders and fields to control various image features. Artbreeder is particularly suited for designers, artists, and anyone looking to develop visual content with AI assistance.

## For whom is Artbreeder suitable?

Artbreeder is geared towards a wide range of users from the design and creative fields, including:

- Artists seeking new inspiration and experimenting with experimental image compositions.
- Graphic designers looking to quickly generate prototypes or concept images.
- Game designers and developers wanting to visualize characters or environments.
- Content creators and social media managers requiring engaging visual content.
- Beginners and hobbyists looking to create without prior knowledge of image editing.

The platform is ideal for users who want to experiment with AI in a simple and intuitive way and enhance their creative projects with unique visual elements.

## Key Features

- **AI-based Image Combination:** Combine and merge multiple images to create unique artworks.
- **Adjustment Sliders:** Control image features such as colors, shapes, styles, and details using intuitive sliders.
- **Portrait, Landscape, and More Generation:** Create various image types, including faces, animals, landscapes, and abstract art.
- **Community Gallery:** Discover and share artworks from other users to gain inspiration or receive feedback.
- **Download Options:** Save your creations in different resolutions, depending on the chosen plan.
- **Freemium Model:** Free usage with basic features and limited storage capacity, as well as paid upgrades for enhanced features.
- **Integration of External Images:** Upload your own images to combine with AI-powered features.
- **Versioning and History:** Manage multiple versions of your artworks and revert to previous states as needed.
