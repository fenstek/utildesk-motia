# Tasker (Android)

Tasker is a powerful automation app for Android devices that allows users to automate a wide range of tasks and workflows on their smartphone or tablet. By combining conditions, actions, and events, users can create custom workflows that simplify everyday life and increase productivity. Tasker is especially popular with users who want to adapt their Android device flexibly to their needs.

## Who is Tasker suitable for?

Tasker is aimed at technically experienced Android users who want to customize their smartphones or tablets far beyond the standard functions. The app is ideal for:

- Users who want to automate repetitive tasks to save time.
- Tech enthusiasts who want to create complex workflows with multiple conditions and actions.
- Professionals who want to boost their productivity through personalized automations.
- Developers and power users who want deep control over their device.
- Users who want to customize their device without root access.

Because of its extensive features and comparatively steep learning curve, Tasker is less suitable for beginners who want simple automations without a period of familiarization.

## Main features

- **Context-based automation:** Tasker responds to numerous triggers such as time, location, Wi-Fi connection, battery status, calls, SMS, and app status.
- **Creation of complex profiles:** Users can define profiles with multiple conditions (e.g. "If it is 6 p.m. and I am at home").
- **Versatile actions:** From controlling settings (Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, volume) to launching apps and sending messages or playing media.
- **Task and scene editor:** Create multi-step workflows and custom user interfaces.
- **Plugin support:** Integration with third-party plugins expands functionality.
- **Export & import:** Profiles and tasks can be backed up, shared, or imported.
- **No root required:** Tasker works on most devices without root, but offers advanced options on rooted devices.
- **Variables and conditions:** Use variables and logic (If/Else) to enable dynamic automations.
- **Tasker App Factory:** Ability to create your own automation apps.

## Advantages and disadvantages

### Advantages

- Very extensive and flexible automation options.
- Large community with many tutorials, examples, and plugins.
- Works without root while still offering deep system integration.
- Regular updates and active developer support.
- Enables complex, multi-step workflows with conditions and variables.
- Highly customizable, from a simple task to your own app.

### Disadvantages

- Steep learning curve for beginners.
- The user interface sometimes feels dated and not very intuitive.
- No permanently free usage model - paid.
- Setting up complex tasks can sometimes be time-consuming.
- No official German-language documentation (mainly English).

## Pricing & costs

Tasker is paid and offered as a one-time purchase in the Google Play Store. There is no permanently free version, although the app can usually be tried before purchase via a trial version or money-back guarantee. Price and availability may vary by region.

## Tasker alternatives

- **Automate:** Also a very flexible automation app with a flowchart-based user interface that is partly easier to use.
- **MacroDroid:** Offers a user-friendly interface and many templates, ideal for beginners.
- **IFTTT (If This Then That):** Focuses on cloud-based automations with many supported services, less deeply integrated into Android system functions.
- **Llama:** A free app for location-based automation, but with more limited features.
- **Easer:** Open-source alternative focused on local automation, but less extensive than Tasker.

## FAQ

**1. Do I need root access to use Tasker?**  
No, Tasker works on most devices without root. However, root access can enable advanced features.

**2. Is Tasker suitable for beginners?**  
Tasker has a steep learning curve. For simple automations, there are more user-friendly alternatives, but with some familiarization, beginners can still succeed.

**3. What kinds of automations are possible with Tasker?**  
Almost all kinds, for example time-based actions, location-based control, app interactions, system control, and more.

**4. Is there a free version of Tasker?**  
Tasker is paid, but sometimes offers a trial version or money-back guarantee. There is no permanently free version.

**5. Can I create my own apps with Tasker?**  
Yes, with Tasker App Factory, users can export their automations as standalone apps.

**6. Does Tasker support third-party plugins?**  
Yes, Tasker supports numerous plugins that enable additional functions and integrations.

**7. Is Tasker equally usable on tablets and smartphones?**  
Yes, Tasker works on all common Android devices, including tablets.

**8. How can I back up or share my Tasker profiles?**  
Tasker allows profiles and tasks to be exported as files that can be backed up or shared with other users.
