# Zoho People

Zoho People is a comprehensive cloud-based HR software solution that helps companies manage their human resources and people operations efficiently. The platform combines automation, self-service features, and AI-powered tools to optimize HR processes such as time tracking, leave management, performance management, and employee data administration. Zoho People is designed for companies of different sizes and industries that want to digitize and simplify their human capital management tasks.

## Who is Zoho People suitable for?

Zoho People is especially well suited for small and medium-sized businesses looking for a flexible and scalable HR solution. HR departments in larger companies also benefit from its modular structure and integration with other Zoho products as well as third-party tools. The platform is ideal for organizations that want to automate their people operations and reduce manual administrative tasks. Zoho People also supports HR teams in industries that value compliance, data protection, and transparent employee administration.

## Key Features

- **Employee master data management:** Centralized and secure storage of all employee information.
- **Time tracking and attendance management:** Automated tracking of working hours, breaks, and overtime.
- **Leave and absence management:** Self-service portal for leave requests, approvals, and absence overviews.
- **Performance management:** Goal setting, feedback, and reviews for employee development.
- **Onboarding and offboarding:** Structured processes for employee onboarding and departures.
- **Workflow automation:** Customizable approval processes and notifications.
- **Reports and analytics:** Detailed HR reports for workforce planning and analysis.
- **Mobile app:** Access HR functions on the go.
- **Integration:** Interfaces to other Zoho applications and external tools such as payroll or ERP systems.
- **Security and compliance tools:** Data protection features and compliance with legal requirements.

## Advantages and Disadvantages

### Advantages

- Intuitive user interface with a high level of ease of use
- Extensive automation and self-service options
- Flexible adaptation to different company sizes and needs
- Mobile app for greater flexibility in HR management
- Good integration with the Zoho ecosystem and third-party tools
- Comprehensive reporting and analytics features
- Regular updates and enhancements

### Disadvantages

- Some features are only available in higher-priced plans
- Getting started can take some time when workflows are complex
- Support and documentation are partly available only in English
- Customization may require additional technical knowledge for specialized requirements

## Pricing & Costs

Zoho People is offered on a subscription model. Costs vary depending on the plan selected and the number of users. There are different pricing tiers ranging from basic features to advanced HR tools. Some plans offer free trial periods so the software can be evaluated in advance. For exact pricing, it is recommended to consult the official website or request a custom quote.

## Alternatives to Zoho People

## FAQ

**1. Is Zoho People suitable for small businesses?**  
Yes, the platform is scalable and offers suitable features and pricing plans for small and medium-sized businesses as well.

**2. Which integrations does Zoho People support?**  
Zoho People can be integrated with other Zoho applications as well as various third-party tools, including payroll systems and ERP platforms.

**3. Is there a mobile app for Zoho People?**  
Yes, Zoho People offers a mobile app for iOS and Android, allowing employees and HR teams to access important functions on the go.

**4. How secure is data in Zoho People?**  
Zoho relies on modern security standards, including data encryption and compliance with data protection regulations, to ensure the security of employee data.

**5. Can Zoho People be tested before purchase?**  
Depending on the plan, Zoho offers free trial periods so the software can be evaluated in advance.

**6. Which languages are supported?**  
The user interface is available in multiple languages, although the availability of support and documentation may vary by language.

**7. How flexible is workflow customization?**  
Zoho People allows approval processes and workflows to be customized, but more complex adjustments require technical know-how.

**8. Which pricing models are available?**  
Zoho People is primarily offered on a subscription model, with different pricing tiers depending on the range of features and number of users.
