# WriterDuet

WriterDuet is a web-based software solution developed specifically for screenwriters and creatives. The tool enables real-time collaborative writing, making it ideal for teams working together on film, television, or theater projects. With a clear, intuitive interface and extensive features, WriterDuet supports the entire writing process, from the first idea to the final screenplay.

## Who is WriterDuet suitable for?

WriterDuet is aimed primarily at professional screenwriters, film students, and creatives who write screenplays, plays, or other narrative texts. It is especially well suited for teams that want to collaborate regardless of location, since the platform offers real-time synchronization. Solo writers also benefit from the organization and formatting features that simplify the writing process.

## Key Features

- **Real-time collaboration:** Multiple users can work on the same document at the same time.
- **Cloud storage:** Automatic saving and access from different devices.
- **Standardized formatting:** Support for all common screenplay standards (e.g., Hollywood format).
- **Offline mode:** Writing is also possible without an internet connection, with synchronization when reconnected.
- **Versioning:** Clear management of changes and previous versions.
- **Import and export:** Support for formats such as PDF, Final Draft, Fountain, and more.
- **Commenting function:** Easy addition of notes and feedback within the text.
- **Outline and structure tools:** Tools for planning and organizing scenes and chapters.
- **Mobile apps:** Access and editing are also available on smartphones and tablets.
- **Security features:** Encryption and data protection according to current standards.

## Pricing & Costs

WriterDuet offers a freemium pricing model. The base version is free and allows limited writing and collaboration. For advanced features such as unlimited projects, offline use, advanced export options, and team features, there are various paid subscriptions. Prices vary depending on the plan and term. More detailed information can be found on the official website.
