# Narrative Science

Narrative Science is a powerful AI software that specializes in the automatic creation of reports and data-driven narratives. The technology turns complex data into understandable, well-structured text that is easy to interpret. This helps Narrative Science support companies in making data-driven decisions and automating their reporting.

## Who is Narrative Science suitable for?

Narrative Science is aimed at companies and organizations that have large amounts of data and want to turn it into understandable reports or stories. The tool is especially suitable for:

- Data analysts and business intelligence teams that want to improve how they communicate data.
- Financial service providers that need automated financial reports.
- Marketing and sales teams that want to generate data-based content quickly.
- Media companies that want to create news and articles from raw data.
- Companies that want to make their reporting processes more efficient and reduce manual text work.

## Main features

- Automatic text generation from structured data.
- Customizable templates for individual report creation.
- Integration with various data sources and BI tools.
- Multilingual text output (depending on the plan and provider).
- Real-time generation of reports and analyses.
- User-friendly interface for easy operation.
- Ability to turn complex data into easy-to-understand narratives.
- API access for developers to integrate into existing systems.
