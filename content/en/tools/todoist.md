# Todoist

Todoist is a versatile, cloud-based task and project management app that helps individuals and teams improve their productivity and organization. With an intuitive interface and smart features, Todoist makes it easy to capture, plan, and track tasks anywhere, anytime. The integration of AI elements makes it easier to prioritize and automate workflows, saving time and increasing focus on what matters most.

## Who is Todoist suitable for?

Todoist is aimed at a broad range of users: from individuals who want to organize their daily to-dos better, to students structuring their study plans, to small and medium-sized teams that want to coordinate projects efficiently. The tool is especially well suited for anyone looking for a flexible, cross-platform solution that can be easily integrated into existing workflows. People who value a clear overview and smart reminder features also benefit from Todoist.

## Main Features

- **Task management:** Create, organize, and prioritize tasks with due dates and labels.
- **Project management:** Set up projects to categorize tasks and keep everything organized.
- **Recurring tasks:** Automatically schedule recurring to-dos, e.g. daily, weekly, or monthly.
- **Smart reminders:** Notifications based on time or location.
- **AI-supported planning:** Suggestions for prioritization and scheduling through machine learning (depending on the plan).
- **Collaboration:** Work together on projects with comments and task assignments.
- **Integration:** Connect with calendars, email clients, voice assistants, and other productivity tools.
- **Cross-platform:** Available as a web app, desktop app, and mobile app (iOS, Android).
- **Productivity stats:** Visualize progress and productivity trends.
- **Offline access:** Tasks can also be managed without an internet connection.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Intuitive and clear user interface.
- Extensive features even in the free version.
- Flexible system for individual and team needs.
- Cross-platform availability.
- Strong integration with external tools and services.
- AI features support efficient planning and prioritization.

### Cons

- Some advanced features are only available in paid plans.
- For very complex project management requirements, it may be less suitable than specialized tools.
- Learning curve when using all options and integrations.
- Privacy concerns depending on usage and data storage location.

## Pricing & Costs

Todoist offers a freemium model. The basic version is free and includes many core features. For advanced features such as enhanced reminders, productivity reports, team functions, and AI-supported planning, there are several paid plans billed monthly or annually. Exact prices vary by region and selected plan. Enterprise solutions with advanced admin and security features are also available.

## Alternatives to Todoist

- **Microsoft To Do:** Free, simple task management with integration into the Microsoft ecosystem.
- **Asana:** Comprehensive project management tool for teams with a wide range of features.
- **Trello:** Visual Kanban board system, ideal for flexible task planning.
- **ClickUp:** All-in-one work management platform with many customization options.
- **Any.do:** Task and calendar app focused on ease of use and daily planning.

## FAQ

**1. Is Todoist free to use?**  
Yes, Todoist offers a free basic version with essential features for individuals.

**2. Which platforms does Todoist support?**  
Todoist is available on the web, Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android.

**3. Is there a team feature?**  
Yes, Todoist enables collaboration on projects, task assignments, and comments, especially in the paid plans.

**4. How does AI help in Todoist?**  
AI features help prioritize tasks and provide smart planning suggestions, depending on the selected subscription.

**5. Can I connect Todoist with other apps?**  
Yes, Todoist can be integrated with calendars, email services, voice assistants, and many other tools.

**6. How secure is my data in Todoist?**  
Todoist uses common security standards; details about data protection can be found on the provider's website.

**7. Is there an offline feature?**  
Yes, tasks can also be edited without internet access and synchronized later.

**8. How can I contact support?**  
Depending on the plan, support can be reached by email or through the help center on the website.
