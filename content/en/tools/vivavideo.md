# VivaVideo

VivaVideo is a popular mobile video editing app designed specifically for users who want to create and edit videos quickly and easily on their smartphone. With a user-friendly interface and a wide range of features, VivaVideo enables both beginners and advanced creators to produce creative and professional videos. The app offers a broad selection of editing tools, effects, and templates that make it easier to create videos for social media, presentations, or personal projects.

## Who is VivaVideo for?

VivaVideo is primarily aimed at mobile users who want to edit videos easily and flexibly. The app is ideal for:

- **Content creators and influencers** who produce regular content for platforms such as Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube.
- **Beginners and hobby filmmakers** who want to create appealing videos without much effort.
- **Teachers and students** who need videos for classroom use or presentations.
- **Travelers and everyday filmmakers** who want to cut and share clips quickly while on the go.

Thanks to its intuitive controls, VivaVideo is suitable both for users with no prior experience and for those who want to gain their first experience with mobile video editing.

## Main features

- **Cut and trim video:** Simple tools for shortening, splitting, and arranging video clips.
- **Effects and filters:** A large selection of visual effects, color filters, and transitions.
- **Text and stickers:** Add text, subtitles, and animated stickers.
- **Music and audio:** Integrate background music, sound effects, and voiceover recordings.
- **Create slideshows:** Automatic creation of video slideshows from photos.
- **Speed control:** Slow motion and time lapse for creative effects.
- **Templates and themes:** Prebuilt designs for fast video production.
- **High-quality export:** Videos can be saved and shared in different resolutions.
- **Social sharing:** Direct publishing to social networks and messaging apps.
- **Cloud synchronization:** Depending on the plan, projects can be synced across devices.

## Pricing & costs

VivaVideo uses a **Freemium** pricing model. The basic features are available free of charge, but with limitations such as a watermark and ads. For full access to all features, ad-free use, and advanced export options, VivaVideo offers various subscription plans. Prices vary depending on the region and the selected term.

- **Free:** Basic features with watermark and ads.
- **Subscription:** Monthly or annual plans that offer additional effects, HD export without a watermark, and premium content.
- **Custom quote:** Special terms may be available for businesses or professional users.

## FAQ

**1. Is VivaVideo free to use?**  
Yes, VivaVideo offers a free basic version with limited features, a watermark, and ads. A subscription is required for advanced features.

**2. On which devices can VivaVideo be used?**  
VivaVideo is primarily available as a mobile app for iOS and Android and is optimized for smartphones and tablets.

**3. Can videos be exported in HD?**  
HD export is often limited in the free version. Full access to HD export and export without a watermark is included in the paid plans.

**4. Which video formats does VivaVideo support?**  
VivaVideo supports common video formats typically used on mobile devices, such as MP4. Details may vary depending on the app version.

**5. Is there a desktop version of VivaVideo?**  
VivaVideo is primarily designed for mobile devices. A desktop version is not currently available.

**6. How secure is my data with VivaVideo?**  
VivaVideo collects certain user data in accordance with the provider's privacy policy. Users should review this before use.

**7. Can VivaVideo be used offline?**  
Many features can be used offline, but some features such as cloud synchronization or certain effects require an internet connection.

**8. Does VivaVideo support multi-track editing?**  
VivaVideo offers basic multi-track functionality, but it is not as extensive as professional desktop software.
