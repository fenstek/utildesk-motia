# Corel Painter

Corel Painter is a professional digital painting and illustration software designed for artists and designers seeking realistic brushes and creative freedom. With a wide range of brush options and AI-powered features, Corel Painter provides a natural and intuitive painting experience on the desktop. The software is suitable for anyone who wants to create high-detail digital artworks with a high artistic standard.

## Who is Corel Painter for?

Corel Painter is ideal for professional artists, illustrators, concept artists, and designers who value a realistic painting experience. Also, advanced hobbyists who want to build their digital painting skills can benefit from the extensive toolset. The software is perfect for users who work on desktop systems and value precise control and a wide range of creative possibilities.

## Key Features

- Realistic brushes and textures that mimic traditional painting techniques
- AI-powered painting tools to support creative processes
- Adjustable workspaces and user interface for individual workflows
- Support for graphics tablets and stylus input devices with pressure sensitivity
- Level and mask functions for complex illustrations
- Color management and color harmony tools
- Import and export options for various image formats
- Time-lapse feature to record the painting process
- Integration of 3D textures and pattern libraries
- Compatibility with Windows and macOS desktop systems
