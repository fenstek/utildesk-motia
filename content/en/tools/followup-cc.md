# FollowUp.cc

FollowUp.cc is an intelligent tool for automating and optimizing email follow-ups. It helps users keep track of important emails by automatically planning reminders and follow-up actions. Especially in the professional sphere, FollowUp.cc helps increase productivity and efficiency in email management.

## For whom is FollowUp.cc suitable?

FollowUp.cc is primarily aimed at professionals who send and receive a large number of emails on a regular basis, such as sales representatives, project managers, customer support staff, and freelancers. Also, companies and teams that want to structure their communication and don't want to miss important follow-ups will benefit from the automation features. The tool is ideal for anyone who wants to make their email communication more professional and time-saving.

## Main Features

- Automatic reminders for unanswered emails
- Planning of follow-up emails directly when sending
- Integration with popular email clients (e.g. Gmail)
- Intelligent scheduling and scheduling function
- Personalized follow-up templates for quick use
- Clear dashboard view for all open follow-ups
- Team features for collaborative management
- Mobile app and browser extensions for flexible access
- Analytics functions for tracking email response rates
- Customizable notifications and prioritization
