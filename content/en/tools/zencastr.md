# Zencastr

Zencastr is a cloud-based tool designed specifically for recording podcasts and interviews in high quality. It offers synchronized audio and video recording, automatic transcription, and helps streamline content production. With built-in automation features, Zencastr supports the productivity of content creators and teams.

## Who is Zencastr suitable for?

Zencastr is primarily aimed at podcasters, journalists, content creators, and teams that want to record remote interviews or conversations. It is ideal for users who value ease of use, high audio quality, and automated post-production. It can also be a suitable solution for educational institutions and businesses that want to record online interviews or meetings.

## Key Features

- **High-quality multitrack recording:** Record multiple participants with separate audio tracks.
- **Video recording:** Synchronized video recordings complement the audio recordings.
- **Automatic cloud storage:** Store recordings securely in the cloud.
- **Real-time audio optimization:** Improve audio quality during recording.
- **Automatic transcription:** Convert audio to text to simplify post-production.
- **Integrated editing tools:** Basic editing options directly in the platform.
- **Integration with other tools:** Connect with editing software and publishing platforms.
- **User management:** Manage team members and access rights.
- **Automated backups:** Protect against data loss through regular backups.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- High audio quality thanks to separate tracks for each participant.
- Easy setup without complicated hardware.
- Cloud-based solution enables flexible work from anywhere.
- Automatic transcriptions save time in post-production.
- Video recording support expands the range of use cases.
- Good integration with common podcast and editing tools.
- User-friendly interface suitable for beginners.

### Cons

- The full feature set is usually available only in paid plans.
- Requires a stable internet connection for optimal results.
- Transcription quality may vary depending on language and accent.
- Limited advanced editing features compared with specialized editing software.
- Data protection and cloud storage can be a concern for sensitive content.

## Pricing & Costs

Zencastr offers different pricing models that vary depending on the feature set and number of users. In general, there is a free basic plan with limited features and paid subscriptions that include advanced features such as unlimited recording time, automatic transcriptions, and higher audio quality. Exact prices vary depending on the provider and plan.

## FAQ

**1. Do I need special hardware to use Zencastr?**  
No, Zencastr is browser-based and only requires a microphone and an internet connection.

**2. How many participants can be recorded at the same time?**  
The maximum number of participants depends on the selected plan; typically, multiple simultaneous recordings are possible.

**3. Are the recordings stored locally or in the cloud?**  
Recordings are automatically stored in the cloud, but they can also be downloaded locally.

**4. Is automatic transcription included in the basic plan?**  
Automatic transcriptions are usually available only in paid plans.

**5. Can I use Zencastr for video podcasts as well?**  
Yes, Zencastr supports both audio and video recordings.

**6. How secure is my data with Zencastr?**  
Zencastr uses common security standards, but you should still review the privacy policy for sensitive content.

**7. Is there a way to edit recordings afterward?**  
Basic editing tools are built in; for complex edits, external editing software is recommended.

**8. Which browsers are supported?**  
Zencastr works best with current versions of Chrome and Firefox.
