# RawTherapee

RawTherapee is a powerful open-source software for editing RAW images. It is designed for photographers and image editors seeking extensive tools to optimize and develop raw data from digital cameras. With a wide range of features, RawTherapee enables detailed and precise image editing—from exposure correction to color enhancement. The software is cross-platform and supports numerous camera models.

## Who is RawTherapee for?

RawTherapee is particularly suitable for hobby photographers, enthusiastic amateurs, and professional photographers who value flexible and free RAW image editing. Because the software offers a variety of technical settings, it is ideal for users who want to delve deeper into image development. Beginners benefit from the opportunity to gradually learn the complex processes of image editing. Due to its open-source nature, RawTherapee is also appealing for users seeking a free alternative to commercial RAW converters.

## Main Features

- Support for many RAW formats from different camera manufacturers
- Non-destructive image editing with adjustment steps that can be changed at any time
- Extensive tools for exposure, contrast, and color correction
- Detailed noise reduction and sharpening functions
- Color management with support for ICC profiles
- Batch processing for editing multiple images simultaneously
- Customizable user interface with multiple layout options
- Tools for local adjustments, such as gradient filters and brushes
- Export to various image formats, including JPEG, TIFF, and PNG
- Support for High-DPI displays
- Integration of histograms and visual analysis tools
- Multithreading to speed up processing on multi-core processors

## Pros and Cons

### Pros
- Completely free and open source
- Very comprehensive and professional editing features
- Cross-platform (Windows, macOS, Linux)
- Regular updates and an active community
- High flexibility through non-destructive editing
- Support for numerous RAW formats
- Good documentation and tutorials available

### Cons
- User interface can seem complex for beginners
- Learning curve needed to use all features effectively
- No integrated Digital Asset Management (DAM) as found in some commercial programs
- Can be slower with very large files or on older computers
- No mobile version available

## Pricing & Costs

RawTherapee is entirely open source and free to use. There are no license fees or subscription costs. The software can be freely downloaded, used, and shared. Users wishing to support development can contribute through donations.

## Alternatives to RawTherapee

- **Darktable** – Another open-source RAW development software with extensive features and a strong focus on workflow optimization.
- **Adobe Lightroom** – Commercial software with a comprehensive feature set and integrated Digital Asset Management, based on a subscription model.
- **Capture One** – Professional RAW development focusing on color quality and workflow, paid with various licensing options.
- **LightZone** – Open-source RAW editor with non-destructive workflow and special exposure tools.
- **digiKam** – Open-source photo management with integrated RAW editing, especially suitable for users wanting to combine image management and editing.

## FAQ

**1. Does RawTherapee support all RAW files from my camera?**
RawTherapee supports a wide range of RAW formats; most common camera models are covered. Support for newer or very specific models may vary depending on the version.

**2. Is RawTherapee suitable for beginners?**
The software offers many professional features, which can make the learning process challenging. However, tutorials and an active community are available to help beginners get started.

**3. Can I edit JPEG or TIFF files with RawTherapee?**
RawTherapee is primarily optimized for RAW files but also supports editing JPEG and TIFF files, though with limited functionality compared to RAW editing.

**4. Is there a mobile version of RawTherapee?**
RawTherapee is currently available only for desktop operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux). There is no mobile version.

**5. How does RawTherapee save my changes?**
RawTherapee works non-destructively. Editing steps are saved in separate files, leaving the original image unchanged.

**6. Can RawTherapee be used with other programs?**
Yes, RawTherapee can be integrated into workflows with other image editing programs, for example by exporting edited images for further processing.

**7. Is there a way to support the software?**
Since RawTherapee is open source, users can support development through donations, feedback, or contributions to the source code.

**8. How often is RawTherapee updated?**
The software is regularly maintained and developed. The frequency of updates depends on the community and the developers.
