# Valohai

Valohai is an MLOps platform specifically designed to automate and manage the entire lifecycle of machine learning projects. The platform helps data scientists and developers train, version, and scale models efficiently, both in the cloud and on-premises. With Valohai, companies can deliver their AI projects faster and more reliably by ensuring repeatable workflows and centralized experiment management.

## Who is Valohai suitable for?

Valohai is aimed primarily at companies and teams that want to integrate machine learning models into production-ready applications and place value on automation, scalability, and reproducibility. In particular, data scientists, ML engineers, and developers who build and manage complex training pipelines benefit from the platform’s features. Organizations that want to run their AI projects in the cloud or in hybrid environments will also find Valohai to be a flexible solution. For beginners in machine learning, the platform may be somewhat complex because of its technical depth.

## Key features

- **End-to-end MLOps platform:** Management of the complete machine learning lifecycle, from data preparation through model training to deployment.
- **Automated pipelines:** Creation and automation of repeatable training and evaluation processes.
- **Version control:** Traceability of models, datasets, and training code through integrated versioning.
- **Cloud and on-premises support:** Flexible deployment and scaling across different infrastructures.
- **Experiment tracking:** Detailed documentation and comparison of training runs and model results.
- **Developer tool integration:** Compatibility with common tools such as Git, Docker, and Kubernetes.
- **Scalability:** Support for distributed training and use of GPU and TPU resources.
- **Security and compliance:** Management of access rights and adherence to company policies.
- **API access:** Programmatic control and automation through a comprehensive API.
- **User-friendly dashboard:** Clear visualization of projects, pipelines, and resources.

## Alternatives to Valohai

- **MLflow:** Open-source platform for managing the machine learning lifecycle, with a focus on experiment tracking and model management.
- **Kubeflow:** Open-source project for building portable, scalable ML workflows on Kubernetes.
- **Weights & Biases:** Cloud-based platform for experiment tracking, model monitoring, and collaboration.
- **SageMaker (AWS):** Fully managed service from Amazon for building, training, and deploying ML models.
- **Neptune.ai:** Tool for experiment tracking, model versioning, and team collaboration.
