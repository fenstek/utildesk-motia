# 10to8

10to8 is a modern scheduling software designed to simplify booking processes and improve communication between service providers and customers. With intelligent features for automated reminders and calendar integration, 10to8 helps businesses reduce downtime and efficiently manage appointments. The software is particularly suitable for small and medium-sized enterprises looking to digitize their appointment management.

## Who is 10to8 for?

10to8 is primarily aimed at service providers who regularly coordinate appointments with customers, including:

- Healthcare and wellness providers (e.g. doctors, therapists, barbers)
- Consulting firms and coaches
- Educational institutions and trainers
- Event organizers of workshops and seminars
- Small and medium-sized businesses seeking a simple online booking system

The platform is ideal for users seeking a reliable, user-friendly solution with automation features to optimize bookings and minimize administrative burden.

10to8 is most useful for teams and individuals who want recurring work to become more structured and transparent. The value should be judged in a real process where organization, task flow, meetings, documentation, alignment, and reliable routines become not only faster but also easier to explain.

10to8 works best when the start is deliberately narrow: a clear purpose, a limited task or data set, and a review step that exists before problems appear.

## Key Features

- Online booking with real-time calendar view  
- Automated reminders via email and SMS to reduce no-shows  
- Integration with popular calendars like Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCal  
- Customizable booking pages with branding options  
- Management of multiple staff and locations  
- Synchronization with video conferencing tools for virtual appointments  
- Real-time notifications for new bookings or changes  
- Reporting and analytics functions to evaluate appointment data  
- API access for individual integrations  
- Support for multiple time zones and languages

- **Practical run with 10to8:** The tool should be tested against a real weekly workflow with tasks, meetings, handoffs, reminders, and review, so strengths and limits become visible outside a polished demo.
- **Quality control in 10to8:** The team needs a simple way to review time saved, fewer follow-up questions, better handoffs, completeness, and stable routines after use.
- **Handoff with 10to8:** Results, open questions, and decisions should be documented so other roles can continue the work later.

## Advantages and Disadvantages

### Advantages

- Intuitive user interface that is easy to use, even without technical expertise  
- Comprehensive automation features that save time and increase appointment loyalty  
- Flexible customization options for various industries and business sizes  
- Free basic version (Freemium) available for testing  
- Integration with many third-party tools and calendars

- 10to8 is especially useful when a recurring process should no longer depend on one person's private know-how.
- 10to8 helps most when organization, task flow, meetings, documentation, alignment, and reliable routines should be documented and checked instead of explained from scratch every time.

### Disadvantages

- Some advanced features are only available in paid plans  
- SMS notifications can incur additional costs depending on the plan  
- The user interface can become overwhelming for complex appointment schedules  
- Support and additional features vary depending on the tariff

- 10to8 needs clarification before rollout when rules, notifications, data care, and ownership are not agreed together; otherwise side processes appear quickly.
- 10to8 saves little when setup, control, and follow-up are expected to happen only on the side.

## Pricing & Costs

10to8 offers a Freemium pricing model that allows for a free trial. Paid plans differ in terms of the number of users, bookings per month, and available add-ons.

- **Free Plan:** Basic features, limited number of appointments and users  
- **Paid Plans:** Subscriptions with expanded features like SMS reminders, more bookings, team management, and priority support  
- **Customized Offers:** For larger enterprises or special requirements, customized solutions are available  

The exact prices vary depending on the scope and chosen plan. For the latest details, please refer to the official website.

The cost of 10to8 is not just the plan price. In practice, number of users, storage, integrations, administration effort, migration, and training also matter because that is where ongoing maintenance and real time investment appear.

## FAQ

Yes, solo entrepreneurs can benefit from the free basic version and simple booking features.

**2. Which calendars are supported?**  
10to8 synchronizes with Google Calendar, Outlook, iCal, and other calendar services.

**3. Can I use 10to8 for virtual appointments?**  
Yes, there are integrations with video conferencing tools like Zoom and Microsoft Teams.

**4. Is there a mobile app?**  
Yes, 10to8 offers mobile apps for iOS and Android to manage appointments on-the-go.

**6. Can I customize the booking form with my branding?**  
Yes, the booking pages can be customized with your logo and colors.

**7. How does the reminder function work?**  
Automated email and SMS reminders are sent before appointments to reduce no-shows.

**8. Is there a trial period for paid plans?**  
Depending on the offer, 10to8 often provides a free trial to test the expanded features.

**9. How should a team test 10to8?**
For 10to8, use one real, bounded use case. Define the goal, owner, data basis, review steps, and success criteria first, then compare effort and output quality after the test.

**10. When is 10to8 a poor fit?**
10to8 is a poor fit when rules, notifications, data care, and ownership are not agreed together, or when nobody has time for setup, review, and ongoing maintenance. In that case the tool quickly becomes another maintenance item.
