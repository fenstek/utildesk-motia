# Anthropic

Anthropic is a cutting-edge AI company specializing in the development of secure and reliable artificial intelligence. The platform offers powerful AI models that can be applied in various application areas, including text generation, analysis, and automation. With a freemium pricing model, Anthropic allows both beginners and enterprises to access modern AI technologies.

## For whom is Anthropic suitable?

Anthropic is geared towards developers, companies, and organizations looking for innovative AI solutions to optimize processes or develop new products. It is particularly suitable for:

- Software developers who want to integrate AI into their applications
- Startups and companies that require automated text processing or analysis
- Research institutions that want to experiment with modern AI models
- Companies that prioritize AI security and ethical standards
