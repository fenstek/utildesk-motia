# Tidio

Tidio is a versatile customer communication platform that combines artificial intelligence (AI) with live chat and chatbots. It helps businesses automate customer service and improve interactions with website visitors. With an intuitive user interface and flexible customization options, Tidio is especially well suited for small and medium-sized businesses that want to make their online communication more efficient.

## Who is Tidio suitable for?

Tidio is aimed primarily at small and medium-sized businesses that want to digitize and automate their customer communication. The tool is especially useful for online stores, service providers, and businesses with frequent customer contact through their websites. Startups and freelancers also benefit from the easy integration and AI-powered features to answer inquiries quickly and generate leads.

## Key features

- **Live chat:** Real-time communication with website visitors to increase customer satisfaction.
- **Chatbots:** Automated responses to frequently asked questions, available around the clock.
- **AI-powered chat automation:** Intelligent recognition of customer inquiries and suitable responses.
- **Multichannel communication:** Integration of email, messenger services, and social media on one platform.
- **Personalized messages:** Customization of chat content based on user behavior and profiles.
- **Mobile app:** Manage chats and notifications on the go.
- **Analytics and reports:** Evaluation of chat performance and customer satisfaction.
- **Easy integration:** Compatible with common CMS and e-commerce platforms such as Shopify, WordPress, or Wix.
- **Multilingual support:** Support for different languages for international customers.

## Pros and cons

### Pros
- Intuitive to use, even for users without technical experience.
- Combines live chat and AI chatbots in one tool.
- Flexible customization of chatbots to match individual requirements.
- Freemium model with a free starting option.
- Multichannel support enables centralized communication.
- Mobile apps keep you reachable on the go.

### Cons
- Advanced features are often available only in paid plans.
- AI chatbots can reach their limits with complex inquiries.
- Limited customization options in the free plan.
- Some integrations require technical know-how.
- Performance can vary depending on website traffic.

## Pricing & costs

Tidio offers a freemium model, where basic features can be used for free. The paid plans vary depending on the provider and usually include expanded chatbot features, additional users, and more communication channels. Prices are usually billed monthly or annually and can be adjusted depending on the number of users and the scope of features.
