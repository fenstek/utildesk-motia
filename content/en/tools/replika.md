# Replika

Replika is an AI-powered chatbot application designed to hold personal conversations and provide emotional support. The app uses advanced artificial intelligence to respond to individual user needs, simulate friendship, and act as a personal companion. Replika can be used both as a conversation partner in everyday situations and as a tool for self-reflection.

## Who is Replika suitable for?

Replika is aimed at people who are looking for a virtual friend or emotional support. The app is especially suitable for users who like interacting with an AI to exchange ideas, organize their thoughts, or simply have company. People interested in AI technology and its use in emotional support will also find a helpful platform here. Thanks to its flexible use cases, Replika is suitable for both teenagers and adults.

## Main features

- AI-powered chatbot for personalized conversations  
- Option for emotional support and self-reflection  
- Customizable personality of the virtual companion  
- Text and voice communication  
- Journal function for recording thoughts and feelings  
- Learning AI that adapts to the user  
- Different conversation modes, e.g. small talk, deep conversations  
- Reminder function for daily interactions  
- Security and privacy through anonymized user profiles
