# Scribens

Scribens is an AI-powered spelling and grammar checking tool that helps users make their texts clearer, error-free, and stylistically appealing. It is suitable for both personal and professional use and supports various text types such as emails, essays, and professional documents. With its intuitive interface and comprehensive features, Scribens helps improve writing quality and productivity.

## Who is Scribens for?

Scribens is aimed at a broad audience: students, authors, professionals, teachers, and anyone who regularly writes texts and values accuracy and style. The tool is especially useful for people who want to check their spelling and grammar quickly and reliably without having to make extensive manual corrections. Non-native speakers also benefit from the clear explanations and improvement suggestions, which promote understanding of the German language.

## Main Features

- **Grammar and spell checking:** Detection and correction of errors in real time.
- **Stylistic improvement:** Suggestions to optimize sentence structure, word choice, and readability.
- **Synonym suggestions:** Alternatives for frequently used words to improve text style.
- **Plagiarism check:** Review of texts for possible plagiarism (available depending on the plan).
- **Multilingual support:** In addition to German, corrections in other languages are also possible.
- **Browser and text editor integration:** Use as an add-on or directly in the web browser.
- **Detailed explanations:** Background information on errors and improvement suggestions.
- **Text statistics:** Analysis of word count, readability, and other important metrics.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Simple and intuitive user interface
- Comprehensive error detection including grammar and style

- Helpful explanations promote language understanding
- Fast corrections directly in the browser or text editor
- Supports multiple languages

### Cons

- Stylistic suggestions are sometimes perceived as subjective
- Not all errors are always detected correctly, especially with complex sentence structures
- Internet connection required for the web version

## Pricing & Costs

Scribens offers a freemium model. The basic version is free and includes fundamental spelling and grammar checks. Paid subscriptions are available for advanced features such as plagiarism checking, more detailed style analyses, and unlimited use. Exact prices may vary depending on the provider and plan.
