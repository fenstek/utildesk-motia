# Pika

Pika is an innovative video tool powered by artificial intelligence that helps users create and edit video content more efficiently. With a user-friendly interface and automated features, Pika is especially well suited for content creators, marketing teams, and companies that want to streamline their video production.

## Who is Pika for?

Pika is aimed at a broad audience, including:

- Social media managers who regularly create short videos for platforms like Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube.
- Marketing teams that want to produce high-quality promotional videos without much time or cost.
- Small and medium-sized businesses that need video content for product presentations or training.
- Individuals or creatives looking for simple, fast video creation with AI support.
- Beginners or users without extensive video experience who want to benefit from automated processes.

## Key features

- **AI-powered video creation:** Automatic generation of video sequences based on text prompts or existing media.
- **Intuitive user interface:** Simple drag-and-drop functions for adding and adjusting clips, text, and effects.
- **Template library:** Access to a wide range of ready-made video templates for different industries and use cases.
- **Automatic subtitles:** Generation and customization of subtitles to make videos more accessible.
- **Music and sound effects:** Integration of royalty-free music and sound effects to improve video quality.
- **Export in various formats:** Support for multiple video formats and resolutions, suitable for different platforms.
- **Cloud-based storage:** Access projects from different devices without local installation.
- **Team collaboration:** Work on video projects together with comments and approval features.
- **Freemium model:** Basic functions are available free of charge, while advanced features can be unlocked for a fee.
