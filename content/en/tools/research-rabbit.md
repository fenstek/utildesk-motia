# Research Rabbit

Research Rabbit is an innovative AI-powered tool that helps researchers, students, and professionals discover, visualize, and analyze academic literature efficiently. By combining data-driven analysis with intuitive visualizations, it provides deeper insight into research fields and makes it easier to find relevant sources.

## Who is Research Rabbit suitable for?

Research Rabbit is aimed primarily at scientists, academics, students, and professionals who regularly work with large volumes of academic publications. The tool is especially useful for:

- Researchers who want to explore new fields of study.
- Students conducting literature research for their theses or projects.
- Teachers looking for current and relevant sources for instruction.
- Data analysts and professionals who want to identify trends and relationships in academic data.

The tool supports users who want to increase their productivity in literature research and better understand complex relationships between studies.

## Key features

- **Visualization of research networks:** Display connections between authors, topics, and publications in interactive graphs.
- **Literature discovery:** Intelligent search and suggestions for related articles based on existing sources.
- **Citation network analysis:** Overview of citation frequency and the influence of studies.
- **Topical clustering:** Grouping of research topics for better orientation.
- **Personalized libraries:** Management and organization of your own literature collections.
- **User-friendly interface:** Intuitive operation even for beginners.
- **Integration with other tools:** Ability to connect to reference managers and databases (depending on plan).
- **Real-time updates:** Automatic updates when new publications appear in relevant fields.
