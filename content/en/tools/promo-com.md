# Promo.com

Promo.com is a cloud-based platform that lets users create professional marketing videos quickly and easily. The software combines user-friendly video editing tools with an extensive library of templates, stock videos, and music. Thanks to integrated AI features, Promo.com especially helps businesses and content creators produce engaging visual content for social media, advertising, and other marketing channels.

## Who is Promo.com suitable for?

Promo.com is aimed at small and medium-sized businesses, marketing agencies, social media managers, and individuals who regularly want to create high-quality videos for promotional purposes but do not have deep video editing expertise. Influencers and content creators also benefit from the time-saving templates and ease of use. The platform is ideal for anyone who wants to produce professional video content with minimal effort to increase brand awareness and customer engagement.

## Key Features

- **Large selection of templates:** Over 100,000 customizable video templates for various industries and occasions.
- **Stock media library:** Access to millions of royalty-free videos, images, and music tracks.
- **Intuitive video editing:** Drag-and-drop editor with text, color, and filter options.
- **AI-powered tools:** Automatic video creation and optimization suggestions based on the content.
- **Branding options:** Add logos, color palettes, and custom fonts.
- **Social media integration:** Direct export in formats for Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and other platforms.
- **Team collaboration:** Work on projects together with multiple users.
- **Multilingual interface:** Support for multiple languages for international teams.
- **Performance analytics:** Track video performance and engagement data (depending on the plan).
- **Mobile app:** Create and edit videos on the go.

## Pros and Cons

### Cons

## Pricing & Costs

Promo.com offers different subscription plans that vary depending on the feature set and usage needs. There is usually a free trial period or a freemium version with limited features. The paid plans usually include:

- **Basic:** Access to basic templates and media, standard export quality.
- **Professional:** Advanced templates, HD export, branding options, and expanded collaboration.
- **Business:** Full access to all features, premium media, analytics tools, and priority support.

Prices vary depending on the billing term (monthly or yearly) and the scope of use. For exact terms, it is recommended to visit the official website or request a custom quote.
