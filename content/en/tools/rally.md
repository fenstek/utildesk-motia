# Rally

Rally is an advanced software solution developed specifically for agile project management in enterprises. The platform helps teams plan, track, and manage complex projects efficiently. With a focus on transparency and collaboration, Rally helps put agile methods such as Scrum or Kanban into practice effectively and increase productivity.

## Who is Rally suitable for?

Rally is aimed primarily at mid-sized and large companies that want to implement agile working methods in their development and management processes. The platform is particularly suitable for:

- Agile teams that want to improve collaboration and transparency
- Project managers coordinating complex projects with multiple teams
- Companies seeking agile scaling at the enterprise level
- Organizations looking for a central solution for planning, tracking, and reporting

The solution is suitable for both IT and software development teams as well as other areas that use agile principles.

## Main features

- **Agile planning and backlog management:** Manage user stories, epics, and features with prioritization and assignment.
- **Sprint and release planning:** Support for creating and managing sprints, including capacity planning.
- **Progress tracking:** Visualize project status using burndown charts, Kanban boards, and dashboards.
- **Team collaboration:** Communication and feedback features to encourage exchange between team members.
- **Reporting and analysis:** Extensive reports for performance measurement and decision support.
- **Scaling agile processes:** Support for frameworks such as SAFe for enterprise-wide agile transformation.
- **Integration:** Interfaces to common tools such as Jira, GitHub, or CI/CD systems.
- **Resource management:** Management of team capacity and task assignment.
- **User and permission management:** Granular control of access rights and roles.
- **Mobile access:** Use the platform on the go via mobile devices.
