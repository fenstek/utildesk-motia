# Speechly

Speechly is an innovative AI-based speech processing solution that enables developers to integrate voice commands and voice interactions into their applications. With a focus on real-time speech recognition and natural language processing, Speechly provides a flexible platform for building interactive, voice-controlled user experiences. The tool is aimed primarily at companies and developers who want to add voice control to their applications without relying on external, often inflexible voice assistants.

## Who is Speechly suitable for?

Speechly is ideally suited for software developers, product managers, and companies that want to integrate voice-based features into web or mobile applications. It is especially useful for:

- Developers who want to build their own voice interfaces.
- Teams that need highly accurate real-time speech recognition.
- Companies that want to improve their user experience through voice interactions.
- Projects looking for flexible and customizable speech processing solutions.
- Developers seeking a combination of speech recognition and natural language processing (NLP).

Thanks to its freemium pricing structure, Speechly is also attractive for small teams and startups that want to start experimenting with voice features.

## Key features

- **Real-time speech recognition:** Detects and processes speech in real time, ideal for interactive applications.
- **Natural language processing (NLP):** Analyzes and interprets voice commands to control applications.
- **Multilingual support:** Supports multiple languages, depending on availability and plan.
- **SDKs and APIs:** Extensive software development kits and APIs for easy integration into various platforms (web, iOS, Android).
- **Flexible customization:** Ability to adapt your own speech models and vocabularies.
- **Offline functionality:** Depending on the plan and implementation, support for offline speech recognition.
- **Custom commands:** Creation and training of specific voice commands for individual applications.
- **Cloud-based infrastructure:** Scalable and reliable servers for fast processing.
- **Analytics:** Insights into the usage and performance of voice-based features.
