# Thunkable

Thunkable is a no-code platform that lets users build mobile apps without programming knowledge. With an intuitive drag-and-drop interface, users can develop both simple and complex applications for iOS and Android. Thunkable offers many features, especially for automation, productivity, and education, that simplify and speed up the development process.

## Who is Thunkable for?

Thunkable is aimed at beginners and advanced users who want to create their own mobile apps without in-depth programming knowledge. The platform is especially suitable for:

- Teachers and students who want to learn or teach app development in an educational setting.
- Entrepreneurs and startups that want to build prototypes or MVPs quickly without developer resources.
- Creative users who want to turn their ideas into interactive applications.
- Teams looking to improve automation and productivity through custom apps.

## Main Features

- **Drag-and-drop interface:** Visual app creation without code.
- **Cross-platform support:** Apps can be built for iOS and Android at the same time.
- **Live preview:** Changes can be tested in real time on connected devices.
- **API integration:** Support for external services and automations.
- **Built-in components:** Buttons, lists, maps, media, sensors, and more.
- **Community and templates:** Access to templates and examples for faster development.
- **Educational resources:** Tutorials and courses specifically for learners.
- **Team collaboration:** Shared work on projects is possible.
- **Publishing:** Direct publishing of apps to app stores or as a web app.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- No programming knowledge required, ideal for beginners.
- Cross-platform app development saves time.
- Extensive components and integration options.
- Fast prototyping and testing.
- Good support for educational use.
- Active community and many learning resources.

### Cons

- Limited flexibility compared with native development.
- Performance limitations may occur depending on complexity.
- Feature set and pricing structure vary by plan.
- Some advanced features are only available in paid versions.
- Customization for deeper technical requirements is limited.

## Pricing & Costs

Thunkable pricing varies depending on the selected plan and feature set. It usually includes:

- **Free basic version:** With core features and limitations on publishing and branding.
- **Pro or premium plans:** With advanced features such as more API access, team functions, higher user limits, and no branding.
- **Business or enterprise solutions:** For larger teams or professional requirements, often with custom pricing.

Exact prices and terms depend on the provider and current offers.

## FAQ

**1. Do I need programming knowledge to use Thunkable?**  
No, Thunkable is specifically designed for users without programming knowledge and uses a visual drag-and-drop interface.

**2. Can I build apps for iOS and Android with Thunkable?**  
Yes, the platform supports simultaneous development for both operating systems.

**3. Is there a free trial?**  
Thunkable offers a free basic version with limited features that is well suited for trying it out.

**4. How do I publish my app?**  
Apps can be published directly from Thunkable to the respective app stores or delivered as a web app.

**5. Is Thunkable suitable for businesses?**  
Yes, especially smaller teams and startups benefit from rapid prototyping and automation options.

**6. Which integrations are possible?**  
Thunkable supports various APIs and external services, depending on the selected plan.

**7. What about support?**  
There is an active community, extensive tutorials, and support offerings that may vary by plan.

**8. Can I work on projects as a team?**  
Yes, Thunkable offers collaboration features, especially in the paid plans.
