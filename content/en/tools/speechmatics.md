# Speechmatics

Speechmatics is an advanced automatic speech recognition (ASR) platform that enables businesses and developers to convert spoken language into text quickly and accurately. With support for numerous languages and dialects, Speechmatics is especially well suited for use cases in media, research, customer service, and more. The platform offers flexible pricing models, including a freemium option that makes it easy to get started.

## Who is Speechmatics for?

Speechmatics is aimed at businesses and individuals that need reliable and scalable speech recognition solutions. This includes media companies that want to transcribe interviews and broadcasts, developers who want to integrate speech recognition into apps, and research institutions that analyze large volumes of audio content. In customer service, Speechmatics can also be used for automatic analysis of phone calls and for improving service quality. Its simple API integration also makes the tool attractive for technical teams.

## Key Features

- **Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR):** Converts audio and video files into text with high accuracy.
- **Multi-language support:** Recognizes more than 30 languages and dialects.
- **Real-time transcription:** Live streaming transcription for use cases such as webinars or meetings.
- **Customizable models:** Ability to adapt models to specific industries or technical terminology.
- **API access:** Easy integration into your own systems and applications.
- **Support for various audio formats:** Compatible with common formats such as WAV, MP3, FLAC, and others.
- **Security and privacy:** Data processing in line with common data protection standards.
- **Subtitling and timestamps:** Automatic creation of subtitles with precise time references.
- **Freemium model:** Free entry with a limited allowance of transcription minutes.

In addition, there are various pricing plans based on usage volume and the features you need. Prices can vary depending on the provider, plan, and individual customization.
