---# CallMiner Eureka

CallMiner Eureka is a conversation intelligence platform for speech and interaction analytics in customer contact. It is relevant when call centers, support, or sales teams need to detect patterns across many contacts, not just review individual calls.

## Who Is It For?

Eureka fits contact-center leaders, customer experience teams, quality assurance, compliance, and revenue operations. It is most useful with high interaction volume and clear goals for coaching, risk detection, or process improvement.

## Typical Use Cases

- Analyze customer conversations by topic, sentiment, and risk.
- Scale quality assurance and agent coaching.
- Detect compliance-relevant interaction patterns.
- Derive customer-experience issues from speech data.

## What Matters In Daily Work

Daily value depends on connecting analysis with change. Dashboards only matter if they lead to coaching, process adjustments, or quality actions.

## Key Features

- Speech and text analytics for customer interactions.
- Dashboards, search, and categories.
- Quality and compliance workflows.
- Insights for coaching and process optimization.

## Strengths And Limits

### Strengths

- Strong for large contact volumes.
- Makes conversation patterns more measurable.
- Can move QA from sampling to broad analysis.

### Limits

- Implementation needs data access, integrations, and change management.
- Automated sentiment and topic analysis must be validated.
- Worker representation and privacy can be central factors.

## Workflow Fit

CallMiner Eureka should not be introduced as an isolated tool. The better starting point is a bounded workflow with input data, owners, a review step, and a decision about where results move next. For this card, the most natural first test is to analyze customer conversations by topic, sentiment, and risk.

## Privacy And Data

Conversation data is highly sensitive. Consent, retention, roles, masking, employee-facing analytics, and regional privacy rules must be clarified before use.
