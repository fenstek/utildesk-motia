# UiPath

UiPath is a leading robotic process automation (RPA) platform that helps companies automate repetitive and time-consuming business processes. With AI-powered capabilities, UiPath makes it possible to create software robots that perform tasks efficiently and without errors. The platform is aimed at companies of all industries and sizes that want to increase productivity and reduce costs.

## Who is UiPath suitable for?

UiPath is especially suitable for companies that want to automate manual, recurring processes in order to save time and resources. Typical users include large enterprises, mid-sized companies, and IT and business teams that want to optimize processes in areas such as accounting, human resources, customer service, or IT management. Organizations with complex workflows and high automation potential also benefit from the platform. In addition, UiPath is suitable for developers and RPA experts who want to build and customize advanced automation solutions.

## Key Features

- Visual process automation via drag-and-drop without extensive programming knowledge  
- Integration of AI components such as machine learning and natural language processing  
- Automation of desktop, web, and legacy applications  
- Management and monitoring of robots through a central orchestration platform  
- Support for attended (with user interaction) and unattended (fully automated) robots  
- Analytics and reporting tools to optimize automated processes  
- Scalability for small automation projects through to enterprise-wide deployments  
- Extensive security and compliance features  
- Community Edition as well as extensive documentation and support

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

## Pricing & Costs

UiPath pricing varies depending on the provider, plan, and business needs. There are various licensing models based on the number of robots, the type of automation (attended/unattended), and the support level. Subscriptions are often offered monthly or annually. A free Community Edition is available for smaller teams or evaluation purposes. For exact pricing information, it is recommended to contact the provider directly or official partners.
