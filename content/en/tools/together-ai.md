# Together AI

Together AI is a modern AI infrastructure platform designed to make it easier to develop, deploy, and collaborate on AI models. With a focus on efficient use of resources and seamless integration, Together AI helps teams deliver AI projects faster and at greater scale.

## Who is Together AI suitable for?

Together AI is aimed primarily at companies, developers, and research teams that need powerful AI infrastructure. It is ideal for teams that want to train or deploy complex AI models without building extensive in-house hardware. It also provides a solid foundation for startups and smaller companies looking for scalable and flexible solutions. In addition, data scientists and AI researchers benefit from the collaboration and AI model management tools.

## Key Features

- **Scalable AI infrastructure:** Enables training and running AI models on flexible cloud resources.
- **Collaborative development environment:** Supports teamwork through shared projects, versioning, and easy model sharing.
- **Model management:** Clear management of models including versioning and deployment.
- **Integration with common frameworks:** Compatible with well-known AI frameworks such as PyTorch or TensorFlow.
- **Real-time monitoring:** Monitoring of training processes and model performance in real time.
- **Security features:** Protection of sensitive data and access controls for team members.
- **API access:** Makes it possible to integrate the infrastructure into existing applications and workflows.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Easy scalability without investing in your own hardware
- Promotes team collaboration through integrated tools
- Compatibility with many common AI frameworks and tools
- Real-time monitoring and management of AI projects
- Flexibility in use depending on project requirements

### Cons

- Dependence on cloud providers and an internet connection
- Potentially higher costs when using large resources over the long term
- Learning curve for users without experience in AI infrastructure
- Depending on the provider, data protection policies may vary

## Pricing & Costs

## Editorial Assessment

Together AI is a defensible choice when a defined workflow, clear ownership and a limited pilot come together. The decision should rest less on a feature checklist than on whether the team can review results, hand work over reliably and respond to change. Our verdict: a good fit for recurring work with an accountable owner; for a narrow or occasional task, a simpler alternative is usually more sensible.

## Workflow and rollout

A useful start with Together AI begins with one concrete workflow and a small user group. Define the input, expected outcome and manual checkpoint before adding more automation or permissions. Record who approves the result and how a failed step is reversed. A focused pilot makes it clear whether Together AI holds up in daily work or only looks convincing in a demo.
