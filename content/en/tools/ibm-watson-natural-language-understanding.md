# IBM Watson Natural Language Understanding

IBM Watson Natural Language Understanding (NLU) is a powerful AI tool for analyzing and processing natural language. It enables companies to understand text automatically, classify it, and extract important information from it. With a wide range of analysis functions such as sentiment analysis, entity recognition, and keyword extraction, Watson NLU supports data-driven decisions and improves business processes through automation.

## Who is IBM Watson Natural Language Understanding suitable for?

IBM Watson NLU is aimed at companies and developers who want to analyze large volumes of unstructured text data. The tool is especially suitable for:

- Data analysts and data scientists who want to evaluate text data from social media, customer feedback, or documents
- Marketing and sales teams that want to identify sentiment and trends
- Developers who want to integrate natural language processing (NLP) into their own applications
- Companies that want to implement automation in text analysis and classification

Thanks to flexible API usage, Watson NLU scales for both small projects and large enterprises.

## Main features

- **Entity recognition:** Identification of people, organizations, places, events, and more in text
- **Sentiment analysis:** Determination of the emotional tone (positive, negative, neutral) of text passages
- **Keyword extraction:** Automatic highlighting of important terms and phrases
- **Categorization:** Classification of texts into predefined categories or industries
- **Syntax analysis:** Recognition of sentence structure, parts of speech, and dependencies
- **Emotion recognition:** Analysis of emotions such as joy, sadness, fear, or anger in text
- **Language support:** Support for multiple languages for global applications
- **Custom Models:** Ability to adapt and fine-tune analysis models for specific use cases
- **API access:** Easy integration into your own software solutions and workflows
