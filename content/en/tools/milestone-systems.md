# Milestone Systems

Milestone Systems focuses on video management software for professional security environments. It helps organisations connect cameras, manage recordings, review incidents and integrate video with other security systems.

The platform is strongest in installations where flexibility, scale and ecosystem support are more important than a simple camera viewer.
