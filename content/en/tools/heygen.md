Using advanced artificial intelligence, HeyGen generates personalized video and audio content that can be used in areas such as marketing, training, or social media. ## Who is HeyGen for?

HeyGen is aimed primarily at marketing professionals, content creators, trainers, and companies that want to improve their communication with personalized and engaging videos. Freelancers and small teams also benefit from its ease of use and wide range of applications. For users who want to produce video content regularly, HeyGen offers an efficient alternative to traditional video production methods.
