# TravelPerk

TravelPerk is a cloud-based business travel management platform that helps companies plan, book, and manage business trips efficiently. With intelligent features for automating travel and expense processes, TravelPerk offers a modern solution for businesses of all sizes that want to optimize travel and control costs.

## Who is TravelPerk suitable for?

TravelPerk is aimed primarily at small and medium-sized businesses as well as larger companies with regular business travel needs. The platform is suitable for travel managers, accounting departments, and employees who need to organize or expense business trips. TravelPerk is especially useful for companies that value transparency, compliance, and simple integration of travel and expense management.

## Main features

- **Central booking platform:** Access flights, hotels, rental cars, and more with transparent prices and a wide range of options.
- **Automated travel approval system:** Customizable approval processes for travel requests to support internal policies.
- **Integrated expense management:** Automatic capture and allocation of travel expenses to simplify reimbursement.
- **Real-time travel cost control:** Clear dashboards and reports for analyzing and controlling travel expenses.
- **Flexible cancellation and rebooking options:** Adjust bookings when travel plans change.
- **Integration with accounting and HR systems:** Seamless connection to common ERP and finance tools.
- **Mobile app:** Book and manage trips anytime, anywhere from a smartphone.
- **24/7 customer support:** Assistance with bookings and issues around the clock.
