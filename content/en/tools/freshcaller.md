# Freshcaller

Freshcaller is a cloud-based phone system designed specifically for customer service and internal communication. With Freshcaller, companies can manage calls efficiently, coordinate teams, and improve customer service without having to invest in complex hardware. The solution is especially suitable for companies looking for flexible, scalable, and user-friendly telephony solutions.

## Who is Freshcaller suitable for?

Freshcaller is aimed at small to medium-sized businesses that want to manage customer communication professionally while keeping it cost-efficient. It is especially well suited to customer service teams, sales teams, and support departments that need a modern phone system without extensive IT infrastructure. Companies with remote or distributed teams also benefit from cloud-based availability and the wide range of call management features.

## Key features

- **Cloud-based phone system:** Make calls over the internet without traditional phone hardware.
- **Multiple phone numbers:** Get local, international, or toll-free numbers.
- **Call forwarding:** Flexible forwarding to team members or external numbers.
- **IVR (Interactive Voice Response):** Automated voice menus for call routing.
- **Call recording:** Calls can be stored for quality assurance.
- **Call queues:** Manage queues during high call volumes.
- **Reports and analytics:** Evaluate call statistics to optimize support.
- **Integration:** Connect with CRM systems and helpdesk software.
- **Mobile apps:** Make and manage calls while on the go.
- **Team collaboration:** Work together on call notes and tickets.

## Advantages and disadvantages

### Advantages

- Easy setup without hardware investment.
- Scalable and flexible to adapt to company size.
- Intuitive user interface for employees and administrators.
- Extensive features designed specifically for customer service teams.
- Cloud-based, so it can be used regardless of location.
- Integration with common CRM and helpdesk solutions is possible.

### Disadvantages

- Depends on a stable internet connection.
- Costs vary depending on the selected plan and features.
- Very large companies with specific requirements may need customization.
- Some advanced features may only be available in higher-tier plans.
