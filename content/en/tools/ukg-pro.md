# UKG Pro (Ultimate Kronos Group)

UKG Pro is a comprehensive Human Capital Management (HCM) solution that helps companies manage HR processes efficiently. With features for personnel management, talent management, and payroll, UKG Pro provides an integrated platform designed for modern HR needs. The software uses AI technologies to automate workflows and make data-driven decisions easier.

## Who is UKG Pro suitable for?

UKG Pro is aimed at medium-sized to large companies across different industries that want to digitize and optimize their HR, payroll, and talent management processes. The solution is especially suitable for organizations looking for a scalable platform to centrally manage personnel administration, time management, and payroll. Companies with complex compliance requirements and employee development needs also benefit from UKG Pro.

## Key Features

- **Personnel management:** Central administration of employee data, organizational units, and compliance information.
- **Payroll:** Automated wage and salary processing with consideration of legal requirements.
- **Talent management:** Recruiting, onboarding, performance reviews, and career planning.
- **Time and attendance management:** Recording work hours, leave planning, and shift management.
- **Analytics and reporting:** AI-powered evaluations for workforce planning and decision support.
- **Self-service portals:** Employees and managers can manage their own data and submit requests.
- **Mobile access:** Access to features on mobile devices for flexible work.
- **Compliance management:** Support for meeting labor law requirements.
