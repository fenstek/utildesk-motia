# VivaCut

VivaCut is a versatile video editor aimed especially at users who want to edit professional videos on mobile devices or desktop. With a mix of easy-to-use tools and advanced features, VivaCut supports both beginners and experienced video creators in producing engaging videos for social media, presentations, or personal projects.

## Who is VivaCut for?

VivaCut is ideal for content creators, influencers, social media managers, and hobby filmmakers who want to enhance their videos with creative effects, multi-track editing, and AI-powered tools. It also offers a flexible platform for users who want to edit quickly and easily while on the go. Thanks to its freemium pricing structure, beginners can start for free and access professional features as needed.

## Key Features

- Multi-track timeline for precise cutting and arranging clips  
- Support for 4K video exports  
- Extensive collection of transitions, effects, and filters  
- AI-powered features such as automatic scene detection and intelligent editing suggestions  
- Text and title animations with customizable templates  
- Keyframe animations for dynamic video effects  
- Audio editing including background music and sound effects  
- Green screen chroma key for creative compositing effects  
- Integration of stock media and external sources  
- Export options for different platforms and formats  

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Intuitive user interface that appeals to both beginners and professionals  
- Powerful AI features make complex editing steps easier  
- Flexible freemium model with many free features  
- Support for high-resolution videos up to 4K  
- Available across platforms (mobile & desktop)  

### Cons

- Some advanced features are only available in the paid version  
- For highly professional film productions, some specialized tools are missing  
- Performance can vary depending on the device, especially on older smartphones  
- Audio editing capabilities are less extensive than those of specialized audio editors  

## Pricing & Costs

VivaCut offers a freemium model. The basic version is free and includes many useful tools to get started. Premium subscriptions, available monthly or annually, unlock advanced features such as 4K export, ad-free use, additional effects, and AI features. Exact prices vary by region and selected plan.

Yes, VivaCut offers a free basic version with many features. A paid subscription is required for premium features.

VivaCut is available for both mobile operating systems (iOS and Android) and desktop computers.

**3. Does VivaCut support 4K video export?**  
Yes, although 4K export is usually included only in the premium version.

**5. What AI features does VivaCut offer?**

**6. Can I share my videos directly from VivaCut to social media?**  
Yes, the app offers export options and direct upload options to popular social networks.

**7. Is there a desktop version of VivaCut?**
