# MediBang Paint

MediBang Paint is a versatile, easy-to-use software for digital drawing and painting that is especially popular among manga and comic artists. The application offers a broad range of tools and features that help both beginners and experienced illustrators bring their creative ideas to life. The program is available across platforms and supports Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS.

## Who is MediBang Paint suitable for?

MediBang Paint is aimed primarily at hobby artists, manga drawers, and illustrators who are looking for a free or low-cost solution for digital drawing. The software is a good fit for users who value ease of use while still wanting powerful features. It is also an attractive option for students, educators, or content creators who want to create comics or illustrations. Thanks to its cloud functionality, it is also ideal for teams or individuals who want to work on the go.

## Main features

- Extensive brush library with different styles and customizable settings
- Layer management including special manga layers (e.g. screentones)
- Cloud storage for easy access and synchronization across devices
- Templates and panel layouts for comics and manga
- Text tools with various fonts and styles
- Perspective guides and grids to support drawing
- Support for PSD files for import and export
- Community features for sharing work and exchanging ideas with other artists
- Regular updates and feature expansions

## Pros and cons

### Pros
- Free entry with extensive features (freemium model)
- Usable across platforms (Windows, macOS, Android, iOS)
- Specifically optimized for manga and comic artists
- Cloud storage makes it easier to work on multiple devices
- Beginner-friendly interface with many tutorials and help resources

### Cons
- Some advanced features are only available in the paid version
- Performance can be limited on older devices
- The interface can feel cluttered for absolute beginners
- Limited professional features compared with specialized programs

## Pricing & costs

MediBang Paint is basically free to use (freemium). The base version already offers many features that are sufficient for most users. For additional features and benefits, such as removing ads or expanded cloud storage options, paid upgrades are available. The exact prices and contents of the premium plans may vary depending on provider and region.

## Alternatives to MediBang Paint

- **Clip Studio Paint** – Professional software for manga and illustration with extensive tools and subscription as well as one-time purchase options.
- **Krita** – Open-source digital painting program with strong features, especially suitable for illustrators and concept artists.
- **PaintTool SAI** – Lightweight and popular painting program focused on ease of use and good brush quality (paid).
- **Adobe Photoshop** – Industry standard with extensive image editing and drawing tools, available as a subscription.
- **FireAlpaca** – Free and simple painting program with a basic feature set, ideal for beginners.

## FAQ

**1. Is MediBang Paint really free?**  
Yes, the base version of MediBang Paint is free to use. However, there are optional paid upgrades that offer additional features.

**2. Which devices can I use MediBang Paint on?**  
MediBang Paint is available for Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS, so it can be used on PCs, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

**3. Do I need an internet connection to use MediBang Paint?**  
The software can be used offline. However, an internet connection is required for cloud features and synchronization.

**4. Can I open my MediBang Paint projects in other programs?**  
MediBang Paint supports importing and exporting PSD files, so projects can also be further edited in programs such as Adobe Photoshop.

**5. Is there a way to share my drawings online?**  
Yes, MediBang Paint offers community features that let users upload their work and share it with other artists.

**6. How difficult is it to get started with MediBang Paint?**  
The interface is designed to be beginner-friendly, and there are numerous tutorials that make it easier to get started.

**7. What special features does MediBang Paint offer for manga artists?**  
For manga in particular, there are screentones, panel layouts, perspective lines, and a large selection of manga brushes and effects.

**8. Can I use MediBang Paint for commercial projects?**  
Yes, commercial use is possible, but it is recommended to check the license terms of the respective plan.
