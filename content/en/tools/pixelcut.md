# Pixelcut

Pixelcut is an innovative design tool specialized in fast and easy image editing. With its AI-powered technology, it enables users to create high-quality graphics and product photos with minimal effort. Pixelcut is especially well suited for e-commerce, social media managers, and anyone who wants to create visual content efficiently.

## Who is Pixelcut for?

Pixelcut is aimed at individuals and businesses that need professional image editing without deep design knowledge. It is ideal for:

- Online retailers who want to cut out and optimize product images
- Social media managers who want to create engaging posts quickly
- Small business owners and freelancers who want to produce visual content cost-effectively
- Creatives who want to create or improve designs with AI support

## Key features

- **Automatic background removal:** AI-based background removal with just a few clicks
- **Template library:** Numerous design templates for social media, advertising, and more
- **Image optimization:** Adjust brightness, contrast, and colors directly in the tool
- **Batch processing:** Edit and export multiple images at the same time
- **Text and logo integration:** Easy addition of text overlays and brand logos
- **Export in different formats:** PNG, JPG, and other common file types
- **Mobile app:** Design editing also possible on the go
- **AI-powered design suggestions:** Automatic layout and style suggestions based on image content

## Pros and cons

### Pros
- Easy to use, even for beginners

- Extensive templates and design elements
- Flexible pricing model with a free entry option
- Mobile app for working from anywhere

### Cons

- Limited customization compared with professional design software
- Batch processing may be limited depending on the plan

## Pricing & costs

Pixelcut offers a freemium model. The free version includes basic features such as automatic background removal and a selection of templates. A paid subscription is required for advanced features such as unlimited image editing, expanded export options, and access to premium templates. Prices vary depending on the provider and the plan selected.
