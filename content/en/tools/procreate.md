# Procreate

Procreate is a popular iPad app for digital drawing, painting and illustration. It combines a responsive brush engine, layers and a clean interface, making it useful for quick sketches as well as polished artwork.

It is a strong choice for artists who prefer a mobile creative workflow over a desktop-first design suite.
