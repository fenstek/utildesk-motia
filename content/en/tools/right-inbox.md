# Right Inbox

Right Inbox is a powerful email productivity tool designed specifically for Gmail users. It expands the email service with important features such as automatic follow-ups, email scheduling, and templates, making day-to-day work noticeably easier and more efficient. By combining automation with smart features, Right Inbox helps keep the inbox better organized and ensures time-sensitive messages are not missed.

## Who is Right Inbox for?

Right Inbox is aimed at professionals, freelancers, and teams that work heavily with email and want to optimize their communication. The tool is especially well suited for sales teams, customer support staff, project managers, and anyone who sends regular follow-ups or wants to time their emails strategically. It is also a practical solution for people who want to simplify their email communication with templates and automation.

## Key Features

- **Email scheduling:** Messages can be scheduled in advance and sent automatically at the desired time.
- **Automatic follow-ups:** Reminders can be set if no reply is received to an email.
- **Email templates:** Frequently used messages can be saved as templates and inserted with just a few clicks.
- **Send Later with time zone support:** Emails can be scheduled according to the recipient's time zone.
- **Reminders:** Personal reminders for important emails directly in the inbox.
- **Tracking:** Notifications when an email is opened or a link is clicked.
- **Integration with Gmail:** Seamless integration into the Gmail interface without additional software installation.
- **Multiple follow-ups:** Automated follow-up emails can be configured at intervals.
- **Team features:** Shared use of templates and reminders within teams (depending on the plan).
- **Mobile support:** Features can also be used through mobile Gmail apps.

## Pros and Cons

### Pros

- Simple and intuitive integration into Gmail without complex setup
- Saves time through automation of follow-ups and email scheduling
- Improves email tracking with tracking features
- Flexible pricing model with a free basic version
- Supports time zones, which is especially important for international communication
- Team features improve collaboration

### Cons

- Works exclusively with Gmail, no support for other email clients
- Some advanced features are only available in paid plans
- Privacy and security can be sensitive depending on use, since email content is processed
- Not open source, so users cannot customize it themselves

## Pricing & Costs

Right Inbox offers a freemium model. The basic features can be used for free, but with limitations on the number of scheduled emails and follow-ups. For advanced features and unlimited use, there are various subscription plans whose prices vary depending on the feature scope and number of users. More detailed information can be found on the provider's official website.

## FAQ

**1. Is Right Inbox compatible with all Gmail versions?**  
Right Inbox works with most Gmail versions, especially the web version and mobile apps that support Gmail. It can also be used with Google Workspace accounts.

**2. Can I use Right Inbox without Gmail?**  
No, Right Inbox is designed specifically for Gmail and does not support other email clients.

**3. Which features are included in the free version?**  
The free version offers basic email scheduling and a limited number of follow-ups. An upgrade is required for advanced features such as unlimited tracking and team features.

**4. How secure is using Right Inbox?**  
The provider uses standard security measures to protect data. However, users should handle sensitive information carefully, since email content is processed.

**5. Can I automate multiple follow-ups?**  
Yes, Right Inbox allows you to set up multiple automatic follow-ups at defined intervals, depending on the plan you choose.

**6. Is there a mobile app for Right Inbox?**  
There is no standalone app, but the features are available through the Gmail app on mobile devices.

**7. How does email tracking work?**  
The tool sends invisible tracking pixels with the email to detect when a message is opened or a link is clicked.

**8. Can I use Right Inbox in a team?**  
Yes, there are team features that allow sharing templates and reminders, although usually only in the paid plans.
